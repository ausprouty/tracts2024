Have You Heard of the Four Spiritual Laws? EKU, EWE WAHULILAKHO KAMALAKa KANE KE BE WELE?
There are natural laws that control the world in which we live. There are also spiritual laws which determine your relationship with God. Kaliho kamalaka kamasalilanwa kakamiia limala sibala nisio khumenyilemo. Lundi kamalaka ke si Wele Kalumilisia busaangi bwowo ne Wele.
1

Law One

God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

1

LILAKA LINYOA

WELE AKHUSIIMA LUNDI AHAANA LIREKEKHA LIE LIHUUKA KHULWA BULAMU BWOWO.

GOD’S LOVE BUSIME BWA WELE
“God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish, but have eternal life” (John 3:16 NIV). Sikila Wele asiima sibaia mala ahaana Omwana wewe omuteelwa, yeesi yeesi osuubila mu nlye akhatiba ta, nekakhali abe ne bulamu bwa mindali na mindali. (Yohana 3:16)
GOD’S PLAN LIREKEKHA LIA WELE
[Christ speaking] “I came that they might have life, and might have it abundantly” (John 10:10). (Yesu alomaloma) "Necha ndi babe ne bulamu, mala babe nabwo bukali." (Yohana 10:10)
Why aren’t all people experiencing the abundant life? Because… Sebali babandu basi bali ne limanya mu bulamu bukali ta, sikila...
2

Law Two

Man is SINFUL and SEPARATED from God. Thus, he cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for his life.

2

LILAKA LIA KHABILI

OMUNDU ALI OMWONI LUNDI WAYAHUKHASIBWA KHUKHWAMA KHU WELE, KHWAKO SANYALA WAMANYA LUNDI WAKHOLA BUSIIMI BWA WELE NE LIREKEKHA LIEWE KHULWA BULAMU BWEWE TAWE.

MAN IS SINFUL OMUNDU OMWONI
“All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God” (Romans 3:23). Sikila boosi bayonaka lundi babombelelwa ne lukoosi lwa Wele. (Barumi 3:23)
We were created to be friends with God; but we chose to go our own way, and friendship with God was broken. This is what the Bible calls sin. Omundu apang'anwa khuba omusaale owa Wele. Ne arobola khuchia khu bwewe ne busaale bwewe ne Wele bwatikulukha. Elino nilio Elomo eya Wele elaanga eli bubwoni.
This is shown: Lino liekesia
1. When people rebel against God, (1) nga omundu naminyalila Wele
2. when people do not care about God and (2) nga nalekelesia Wele, lundi
3. when people do not live up to God's perfect standard (3) nga nakhamenya wolela sikelo sisichutu sia Wele tawe.
“The wages of sin is death” [spiritual separation from God] (Romans 6:23). Sikila kumuruungo kwe bubwoni kuli lifwa. (liliahukhana lie si moyo khukhwama khu Wele) (Barumi 6:23)
God is holy and man is sinful. There is a great separation between the two. The arrows show how man tries to reach God and the abundant life through his own efforts: such as living a good life, being religious, helping others etc., but all his efforts fail. Wele omulungi ne omundu omwoni. Buliho bubwahukhani bukali akarikari wabwe. Buhembe bwekesia nga omundu nakhaka khukhwolela Wele ne bulamu bukali khu maani kewe: Khumenya limenya lilayi, khuba omwisaayi, khuyeeta babandu babandi nekakaandi, ne kamaani kee koosi kakhilwa.
The third law gives the only answer to this problem… Lilaka lia khataru likhuwelesia likobosia lilala iiong'ene khu butinyu buno...
3

Law Three

Jesus Christ is God’s ONLY provision for man’s sin. Through Him you can know and experience God’s love and plan for your life.

3

LILAKA LIA KHATARU

YESU KRISTO NIYE OLI ENGILA YONG'ENE NIYO OMUNDU ABIRIRAKHO NIO WOLELA WELE. AKHUFWIILA. ONYALA WAMANYA WELE KHUBIRIRA MU NIYE NE KHUMANYA BUSIIMI BWEWE NE NDEKEKHO KHULWE BULAMU BWOWO.

HE DIED IN OUR PLACE AFWA MALA WALAMUKHA KHUKHWAMA MU BAFU
“For Christ died for sins once for all, the righteous for the unrighteous, to bring you to God”. (1Peter 3:18) Sikila Kristo omwene afwa khulwe bubwoni lulala khu banau boosi, omulungi kafwila bakhali balungi ta, nio akhurere efwe fweesi khu Wele... (1 Petero 3:18)
“After his suffering, he showed himself to these men and gave many convincing proofs that he was alive. He appeared to them over a period of forty days and spoke about the kingdom of God.” (Acts 1:3) Mundaalo kamakhumi kane enyuma we lifwa lia Yesu, niye abonekhela babandu bakali mu bise bikali, mu ngila chichokesia chili mu bung'ali mbo Yesu kaba namalile khulamukha khu khwaama mu bafu; Bamubona, mala alomaloma nenabo alubeka we bubwami bwa Wele. (Bikholwa 1:3)
HE IS THE ONLY WAY YESU NIYE ENGILA YONG'ENE
“Jesus said to him, ‘I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father, but through Me.’” (John 14:6) Yesu amubolela ali, "Ese ndi engila, bung'ali ne bulamu; se aliho owicha khu paapa ne akhabirire khwise tawe." (Yohana 14:6)
God has bridged this separation between us and Himself by sending His Son Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place. Omundu khumaani kewe yeng ene, se anyala kbuba ne busanganilo ne Wele Tawe. Wele aruma Yesu afwe khu musalaba sikila bubwoni bwefwe ne lundi nive abe bulalo akari wefwe ne Wele nio akhuambasie efwe ne Wele.
It is not enough just to know these three laws … Lilaka lia khane lila khubolela nga khuli nonyala khumanya Wele mu bulamu bwowo....
4

Law Four

We must individually RECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

4

LILAKA LIA KHANE

BULI MULALA KHWIFWE AKWANIBWA KHU FUKILILA YESU ABE OMUHONIA LUNDI OMWAMI WEWE. ENYUMA WAKO, NONO KHUNYALA KHUMANYA NE KHUULILA BUSIIME BWA WELE NE LIPANGANA LIE BULAMU BWEFWE.

WE MUST RECEIVE CHRIST KHUKWANIRWA KHUFUKILILA KRISTO MWISUBILA
“As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, even to those who believe in His name” (John 1:12) Khwa abo boosi babafukilila Yesu, baba subila mwisina liewe, kabahelesia bunyali bwe khuba babana ba Wele. (Yohana 1:12)
“By grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God; not as a result of works, that no one should boast” (Ephesians 2:8,9). Sikila sie kumwalalo kwa Wele, waonisibwa mwisubila mala lino seliama khwiwe ta; esino sili sihanwa sia Wele - sesili sikila sie biikholwa ta omundu akhakhola ario wefumia tawe. (Baefeso 2:8,9)
WE RECEIVE CHRIST THROUGH PERSONAL INVITATION KHUFUKILILA KRISTO NIKHWO KHUMWIKULILAO KIMILIANGO KIE BULAMU BWEFWE ECHE KHUMENYA MU BULAMU BWEFWE
[Christ speaking] “Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if any one hears My voice and opens the door, I will come in to him.” (Revelation 3:20) (Kristo niye oloma yino) "Lola, nemile khumuliango ndikho khong'onda; omundu yeesi yeesii ohulila kumulango kwase maia wekulao, Ndenglia mu niye." (Lifunula 3:20)
RECEIVING CHRIST INVOLVES ALL OF THE FOLLOWING: Khufukilila Yesu Khuii
1. We are to agree that all of us are sinful, but through Jesus Christ we can be free of sin 1. Khumanya oli ewe omwoni mala weyame bubwoni bwowo khu Wele nekhu bufutarira.
2. We should believe that Jesus Christ died for our sins 2. Yeikina Wele khukhu beela bubwoni bwowo bwoosi.
3. We should believe that Jesus Christ is involved in all our daily life. 3. Khufkilila Yesu amiie bulamu bwowo mwisubira, nio omale khuba nga niye ne Kenya

YOU CAN RECEIVE CHRIST RIGHT NOW BY FAITH THROUGH PRAYER ONYALA KHU FUKILILA KRISTO MU SISE SINO
God knows your heart and is not so concerned with your words as He is with the attitude of your heart. The following is a suggested prayer: Wele amanyile khuli nga liba liowo nelili. Kamakhuwa kalondakho kano mulisaba liowo mu sise esino kanyala kakhuyeta khufukilila Kristo ne kaba bali ola saba ne kumoyo kwa ng'ali.
“Lord Jesus, I need You. I have been controlling my own life and have sinned against You. Thank You for forgiving my sins. Come into my life and take control of my life. Make me the kind of person You want me to be. Amen” Omwami yesu Kristo, ese nakhililwe lukali, kano kakila ne nikomba khu khwolela. Ese ndi omwooni. Fukilila ndi ese samwene namiia bulamu bwange, ne khukhweyaula khukhwama khwiwe. Nasiima nga ne wafwa khu musalaba sikila bubwooni bwange. Niyama ndi ese ndi omwooni ebweni wowo. Yesu mbele mala wingile mu bulamu bwange mu sise esino mala obe omwami we bulamu bwange. Ngalukhasie mbe sibumbe siowo sinyaia, sia kbusiimisia. Nasiima nga nilwo umbelele ne khukhwicha mu bulamu bwange. Amina
Does this prayer express the desire of your heart? Eku, lisaba elino lilondekhana khuli nga ewe wamwene ne wenyile?
If it does, I invite you to pray this prayer right now, and Christ will come into your life, as He promised. Saba mu sise esino mala ofukilile Kristo keche mu bulamu bwowo.
HOW TO KNOW THAT CHRIST IS IN YOUR LIFE KHULI NGA NILWO ONYALA KHU MANYA OLI KRISTO ALIMU BULAMU BWOWO
Did you receive Christ into your life? According to His promise in Revelation 3:20, where is Christ right now in relation to you? Eku, se wama khufukilila Kristo eche Mu bulamu bwowo? Omanya orie ne kechile? Nono Kristo ali waena? Khebulila (Lifunuia 3:20)
Christ said that He would come into your life. Would He mislead you? Yesu aloma ali anyala Khu khwicha mu bulamu bwowo, Eku, akano sekali kang'ali?
On what authority do you know that God has answered your prayer? (The trustworthiness of God Himself and His Word.) Eku, omanya oliena oli Wele aulile lisaba liowo? Wele akhusubisia aliliosi liosi nilio khusaba khu niye mwisubila ala khukholela.
THE BIBLE PROMISES ETERNAL LIFE TO ALL WHO RECEIVE CHRIST EVANGELI ELAKILISIA BULAMU BWA MINDALI KHU BOOSI TAFUKILILA KRISTO
“The witness is this, that God has given us eternal life, and this life is in His Son. He who has the Son has the life; he who does not have the Son of God does not have life. These things I have written to you who believe in the name of the Son of God, in order that you may know that you have eternal life” (1 John 5:11-13). Liboola nilio lino: Wele akhuhelesia etwe bulamu bwe bumelela, mala bulamu buno bwakukha mu Mwana wewe. Omundu yeesi yeesi oli ne omwana ali ne bulamu buno, yeesi yeesi okhali na omwana wa Wele saline bulamu tawe. Ese ndikho mbandika kano khwinywe nio mwole khumanya muli, muli ne bulamu bwe bumelela, enywe mu subila mu mwana wa Wele. (I Yohana 5:11)
Thank God often that Christ is in your life and that He will never leave you (Hebrews 13:5). You can know that Christ lives in you and that you have eternal life because of His promise. Siima Wele njilili sikila Yesu amenyile mu bulamu bwowo. Subila oli Yesu se alikhulekha tawe. Matayo 28:20 Amalile khukhuwa bulamu bwe bumelela. (Heb. 13:5)
DO NOT DEPEND ON FEELINGS OKHEYIKINA KAMEBASIO KOWO TAWE
The Christian lives by believing God and what He says in in the Bible. The promise of God's Word is our authority. Onyala khuba ne busangafu luno ne mukamba obe ne bubeleli. Mungila yoosi yoosi niyo olehulilamo, bubele busangafu bubele bubeleli, Yesu Kristo se ali kalukhana mu bulamu bwowo tawe.
Truth has the power to lead us. Khuambanisibwa ne bunyali bwe bung'ali mwisuubila.
We are joined to the power of Truth by Faith. Nekhulonda bung'ali, khukhwehulila khwe kamebasio (Lisuubila) khuranaililwa bulayi.
As we obey the Truth, our feelings follow along in the right way. Khweyikina Wele ne Bungali bwewe, se khuli khukhweliulila khwe kamebasio (liba lie Kumwoyo) tawe.
We trust God and His Truth, not our Feelings Khulwa ako okheyikina kamebasio kowo (liba lie kumwoyo) ta, ne kakhali, yeyikina bung'ali bwe Elomo eya Wele.
NOW THAT YOU HAVE RECEIVED CHRIST SIKILA WAMALILE KHU FUKILILA YESU
The moment that you received Christ by faith, as an act of the will, many things happened, including the following: Khu khwama nga ne watukilila Yesu Kristo, bibindu bikali mu limenya liowo biamala nebikalukhana.
1. Christ came into your life (Revelation 3:20) 1. Yesu amala ne echa mu bulamu bwowo (Lifunula 3:20)
2. Your sins were forgiven (Colossians 1:14). 2. Wabelwa bubwooni bwono bwoosi (Bakolosai 1:14)
3. You became a child of God (John 1:12). 3. Wamala nokholeha khuba omwana owa Wele (Yohana 1:12)
4. You began the great adventure for which God created you (John 10:10, 2 Corinthians 5:14, 15, 17). 4. Ola chililila khumanya lilienya lia Wele mubulambu bwowo (II Bakorinjo 5:14,15,17)
Can you think of anything more wonderful that could happen to you than receiving Christ? Eku, silikhoho sikholwa silayi sikhila khufukilila Yesu engile mu bulamu bwowo?
Would you like to thank God in prayer right now for what He has done for you? By thanking God, you demonstrate your faith. Khusiime Khwefwe mu ngila eye lisaba khwokesia khuli, khuli ne lisuubila mu Wele. Nono khusabe.

SUGGESTIONS FOR CHRISTIAN GROWTH NGA NILWO ONYALA KHUKHULA MU MUROHO
Spiritual growth results from trusting Jesus Christ. . A life of faith will enable you to trust God increasingly with every detail of your life, and to practice the following: Bulayi bwene nga omwana omutoro, okwanibwa khukhula. Ola chililila bulayi mu limenya lie mu muroho nekaba bali olalonda kamakambilo kalondakho bull njilili.
1. Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7). 1. Kachula nga ne Wele mwisaba. (Yohana 15:7)
2. Read God’s Word daily (Acts 17:11); begin with the Gospel of John. 2. Soma Elomo eye Wele. (Bikholwa 17:11)
3. Obey God moment by moment (John 14:21). 3. Obe omusuubilwa ebweni wa Wele. (Yohana 14:21)
4. Witness for Christ by your life and words (Matthew 4:19; John 15:8). 4. Bolela babandi Khulwa Kristo mu bikholwa bwowo ne liloma liowo. (Matayo 4:19 ne Yohana 15:8)
5. Trust God for every detail of your life (1 Peter 5:7). 5. Yeyikina Wele buli mu sindu mu bulamu bwowo. (1 Petero 5 7)
6. Have God control and empower your daily life and witness (Galatians 5:16 & Acts 1:8). 6. Elesia Roho Omutakatifu bubwangu akhwamiie. (Bagalatia 5:16-18 ne Bikholwa 1:8)
FELLOWSHIP IN A GOOD CHURCH LILI LIE BUKHALA KHU BAKRISTO KHUBA NE BUSANGANILO
God’s Word instructs us not to forsake “the assembling of ourselves together” (Hebrews 10:25) Elomo eya Wele ekhubolela eli mwiche halala (Mwisanga). (Baebrania 10:25)
Several logs burn brightly together; but put one aside on the cold hearth and the fire goes out. So it is with your relationship with other Christians. Lekha khurumikhile sifwani sie kumulilo. Nora chikhu chingali halala chinyala chaburukha bulayi lukali, mala ne obukula lukhu lulala walura alubeka ne lusima.
If you do not belong to a church, do not wait to be invited. Akhaba ewe weesi, sebuli bulayi khumenya limenya lie si Kristo weng'ene tawe.
Take the initiative; call the pastor of a nearby church where Christ is honored and His Word is preached. Sanga mu kanisa nimwo babandu basuubila Elomo eya Wele bali buli bung'ali lundi bafumia Yesu.
