s 5:16,17; Acts 1:8).
Have you heard of the Four Spiritual Laws?Tos Kiigas Agobo Ng’atutik Ang’wan Che Bo Sobet ?
There are natural laws that control the world in which we live. There are also spiritual laws which determine your relationship with God.Mitei ng’atutik che bo emet che ribei ng’onyut ne kimenye. Mitei kora ng’atutik che bo sobet che ribei tilianding’ung’ ak Jehovah.
1

God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

 1

CHAMIN Jehovah ak kotinye TETUTIET ne bo kwong’et agobo Sobeng’ung’.
God's Love CHAMBYETAB JEHOVAH
“God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish, but have eternal life” (John 3:16 NIV). “Amu kicham Jehovah ng’ony mising, kot kogoito werinnyi ne kigii inegei, si malus age tugul ne iyani inendet, ago si konyor sobondab koigeny.” (Johana 3:16)
God's Plan TETUTIETAB JEHOVAH
Jesus said, "I came that they may have life, and have it abundantly" [that it might be full and meaningful]." (John 10:10) [Ng’alali Kristo] “Kianyo si kobiit konyor sobondo, ak kotepto sobonato inne.” (Johana 10:10)
Not all people have experienced this life, because… Tomo konyor bik tugul naet agobo sobonito, amu…
2

People are SINFUL and SEPARATED from God so we cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for our life.

 2

KIPTENGEKYOT chito ak KIGOBESIO ak Jehovah, kou noto ko. Maimuchi konai ak konyor chamnyetab Jehovah ak tetutiet ne bo Sobennyi.
People are Sinful KIPTENGEKYOT CHITO
"All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23) “Amu kigoyai tugul tengek, ama ityin torornatetab Jehovah” (Romanik 3:23)
Man was created to be friends with God; but he chose to go his own way, and friendship with God was broken. This is what the Bible calls sin. This is shown (1) when man rebels against God, (2) when he does not care about God and (3) when he does not live up to God's perfect standard. Kigiyai chito si koek choronok ak Jehovah. Ago kilewen kosub orennyi ak kibutok choruandinnyi ak Jehovah. Nito ne kuure bukuit ne tilil tengekto. Kiboru nito (1) ye kingongelelji chito Jehovah ak (2) ye ma mage kiy Jehovah ak (3) ye ma imuch kosob kot koityi oretab Jehovah ne igesunotgei.
People are Separated KIKOBESIO CHITO AK JEHOVAH
"The wages of sin is death" [spiritual separation from God]. (Romans 6:23) “Amu melektab tengek ko meet” (kabesiet ne bo sobet ak Jehovah Romanik 6:23).
God is holy and man is sinful. There is a great separation between the two. The arrows show how man tries to reach God and the abundant life through his own efforts: such as living a good life, being religious, helping others etc., but all his efforts fail. Tilil Jehovah, ko kiptengekyot chito. Mi kabesiet ne o eng’ tuwai. Iboru kipchabonik tiemetab chito koityi Jehovah ak sobet ne nyitat eng’ kimnatennyi: kosob sobet ne Mie, koyai ne bo imanda, kotoret biik alak, ak alak, ago egu buch kimnatennyi amu maityin Jehovah.
The third law gives the only answer to this problem… Ng’atutiet ne bo somok kogonech wolutiet ne bo iman agobo kaimutyonito…
3

Jesus Christ is the ONLY way man can reach God. He died for us. Through Him you can know God and experience His love and plan for your life.

 3

Jesu Kristo KO ORET NE INGEGEI ne iite chi ole mi Jehovah. Kime agobo achek. Eng’ inendet inae Jehovah ak, inyoru chamnyennyi ak tetutuiennyi agobo sobeng’ ung’.
HE DIED AND ROSE FROM THE DEAD KIME AK KING’EET KOMONG’ ENG’ CHE KIBEK
1. “For Christ died for sins once for all, the righteous for the unrighteous, to bring you to God”. (1Peter 3:18) 1.”Amu kime Kristo agine Konyil agenge agobo tengekwogik, inendet ne bo imanda agobo che ma bo imanda, si kobiit komutiech Jehovah...” (1 Petero 3:18)
2. “After his suffering, he showed himself to these men and gave many convincing proofs that he was alive. He appeared to them over a period of forty days and spoke about the kingdom of God" (Acts 1:3) 2.Icheget che Kiiborjigei Kosobei ye Kingobata nyalildanyi, eng’ kaborunoik che chang’, kongang’jigei ichek eng’ betusiek artam, ak kong’alalJi ichek agobo tuguk che bo bounatetab Jehovah. (Boisionik 1:3)
He Is the Only Way to God ORET INEGEI
"Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father but through Me.'" (John 14:6) “Kolenji Jesu, A oret, ak iman, ak sobondo: Manyonjin chi kwanda ama eng ane.” (Johana 14:6)
God has bridged this separation between us and Himself by sending His Son Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place. Kiteech Jehovah etiet eng’ kwenut ab achek ak Inendet, ye kingoyok werinnyi Jesu Kristo, konyo kome eng’ kimurtoiyot agobo achek.
It is not enough just to know these three laws... Mayamei kenai ng’atutichu somok kityo…
4

We must individually RECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

 4

Nyoljin chi tugul KOTAACH Jesu Kristo koek yetindet ak kiptaiyat; si komuch konai ak konyor chamyetab Jehovah ak tetutiennyi agobo soboennyi.
We Receive Christ Through Faith NYOLJINECH KETAACH KRISTO ENG’ KAYANET
"As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, even to those who believe in His name." (John 1:12) “Ago tugul che kitaach Inendet, ko kiigochi kamuget koek lagokab Jehovah “ (Johana 1:12)
"By grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God; not as a result of works so that no one may boast." (Ephesians 2:8,9) “Amu kigesoruok eng’ mieindo eng’ kayanet; ago mabunwok nito: konunetab Jehovah; ama bo boisionik si mailosgei chi.” (Epesoek 2:8,9)
WE RECEIVE CHRIST BY INVITING HIM INTO OUR LIVES KITAACHE KRISTO YE KAGIGOCHI INENDET BOROINDO KONYO SOBONWEKYOK
[Christ speaking] "Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if anyone hears My voice and opens the door, I will come in to him." (Revelation 3:20) [Ng’alali Kristo] “Keero, atononi eng’ kurgat ak agongonyi: ngo kas chi kutinnyu ak koyat kurget, awendi ole mi inendet…” (Ng’ang’utiet 3:20)
It is not enough just to agree with Christ’s teachings. Receiving Christ means turning to God from self (repenting). We trust Christ to come into our lives and to forgive us our sins. We let Him make us what he wants us to be. Mayamei kiyan konetisietab Kristo kityo. Ketaach Kristo ko keesiegei ak kiweekyigei Jehovah (nito ko sigunet). Kimang’u Kristo konyo sobonwekyok ak konyoiywech kaat tengekwogikyok, kebagakyin inendet kowalech ak koyaech kou ye machei.

God knows your heart and is not so concerned with your words as He is with the attitude of your heart. The following is a suggested prayer:Som Inendet konyo sobondang’ung’. Lord Jesus, I need You. Thank you for dying on the cross for my sins. I open the door of my life and receive You as my Savior and Lord. Thank You for forgiving my sins and giving me eternal life. Take control of the throne of my life. Make me the kind of person You want me to be. “Kiptaiyat Jesu, amakyininge. Kiabou sobondanyu ak kiayaun tengekto. Nyoiywokaat tengekwogikyuk tugul. Kongoi amu kainyoiywo kaat tengekwogikyuk. Nyo sobondanyu ak ibou sobondanyu komugul. Yaa aek chito ne imache aek.” Amen.
Does this prayer express the desire of your heart? If it does, I invite you to pray this prayer right now.Tos iboru saoni magetab muguleldang’ung’?
Christ will come into your life as He promised. Ngot kou noto, isaa saonita nguno, ak nyonei Kristo sobondang’ung’ kou ye kigoyomis.

Would He mislead you?Tos ketaach Jesu Kristo konyo sobondang’ung? Kosupgei ak kayomisutiennyi eng’ Ng’ang’utiet 3:20, ko miano Kristo nguno?
On what authority do you know that God has answered your prayer? (The trustworthiness of God Himself and His Word.)Kimwa Kristo kole nyone sobondang’ung. Tos imuchi komwaun lembech? Inaitoi ano ile kagowol Jehovahsaeng’ung’? (Amu imang’u Jehovah ak Ng’olyondennyi)

These things I have written to you who believe in the name of the Son of God, so that you may know that you have eternal life." (1 John 5:11-13)“Kigonech Jehovah sobondab koigeny, ak mi sobonito Werinnyi. Inendet ne tinyei werit kotinyei sobondo inendet ne matinyei weritab Jehovah ko matinyei sobondo. Kaasirwak tuguchu, si kobit onai ole otinye sobondab koigeny, okwek che oyani kainetab weritab Jehovah.” (I Johana 5:11-13)
Thank God often that Christ is in your life and that He will never leave you (Hebrews 13:5).   • Kanyo Kristo sobondang’ung’ (Ng’ang’utiet 3:20 ak Kolosaik 1:27)

SUGGESTIONS FOR CHRISTIAN GROWTHOLE KINERTOI ENG’ SOBET NE BO TAMIRMIRIET
Spiritual growth results from trusting Jesus Christ.Kou lakwet ne ming’in konyolu iner:
  • Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7)
  • Ui taitab Jehovah eng’ saet kotugul. (Johana 15:7)
  • Read God's Word daily. (see Acts 17:11) Begin with the Gospel of John.
  • Soman Ng’olyotab Jehovah kotugul (Boisionik 17:11) Taune bukuitab Johana.
  • Obey God moment by moment. (see John 14:21)
  • Kas it Jehovah kotugul. (Johana 14:21)
  • Witness for Christ by your life and words. (see Matthew 4:19 and John 15:8)
  • Baorian Kristo eng’ sobeng’ung’ ak eng’ ng’aleguk. (Matayo 4:19 ak Johana15:8)
  • Trust God for every detail of your life (1 Peter 5:7).
  • Imang’u Jehovah eng’ kebeberta age tugul ne bo sobeng’ung’. (1 Petero 5:7)
  • Holy Spirit – allow Him to control and empower your daily life and witnes
  • Igochi Jehovah koboi ak kogimit sobeng’ung’ akbaornateng’ung ne bo kotugul. (Galatiek 5:16,17 ak Boisionik 1:8)
