2

LAW TWO

People are SINFUL and SEPARATED from God so we cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for our life.

 2

Kɔc acï kërac looi kedhie ku puɔ̈ki ke Nhialic. Yen akuc wuɔk Nhialic ku kuc nhiëër de Nhialic, ku kä cïï Nhialic guiir në baŋ de pïïrda.
People are Sinful Raan acï kërac looi:
"All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23) “Kɔc acïk kërac looi kedhie ku dëëkkï dhuëëŋ de Nhialic.” (Romai 3:23)
We were created to be friends with God; but we chose to go our own way, and friendship with God was broken. This is what the Bible calls sin. This is shown: Raan e ciek Nhialic bikï ya muäth. Go raan rot yal wei ku leer në kueerdɛɛn pei, go määthden kekë Nhialic riääk. Riääk de määth; yen ki Baibol cɔl kërɛɛc cï raan looi.Ku ee tïc në:
when people rebel against God; Reec cïï raan Nhialic rɛɛc thok ë pïŋ; ku
when people do not care about God; and cïï löŋ de Nhialic ye nëu; ku
when people do not live up to God's perfect standard. cïï pïïr lɔgɔl de Nhialic piɔ̈u looi.
People are Separated Puɔ̈k Nhialic kek Raan:
"The wages of sin is death" [spiritual separation from God]. (Romans 6:23) “Ariöpde kërac yen aye thuɔɔu" [Pɔ̈k de wëi ke raan në Nhialic]. (Romai 6:23)
God is holy and people are sinful. There is a great separation between the two. The arrows show how people try to reach God and the abundant life through their own efforts: such as living a good life, being religious, helping others etc., but all their efforts fail. Ë wiethkë anyoth thëm ee raan lɔ tëde Nhialic them ë guɔ̈pde, në ka cït man de pïir pieth, ku jam de wël ke pelënɔm, ku cieeŋ pieth, ku ye rot tɔ̈ ye raan de Nhialic, ku ye kɔc kuany nyin kuɔny, ku ka kɔ̈c cït ë kakë. Ku kakɛɛn cï them ë luɔi kedhie, acïk löny.

LAW THREE

Jesus Christ is the ONLY way people can reach God. He died for us. Through Him you can know God and experience His love and plan for your life.

 3

Yiny de Diäk

Yecu Kritho yen e kuer yetök , kueer lëu ë raan lɔ tëde Nhialic. Acï thou në biäkda. Në yen, yïn lëu ba Nhialic ŋic ku ŋic nhiëërde ku cäpdït cï guiir në baŋ de pïirdu.
He Died and Rose From the Dead Acï thou ku jɔt rot në thuɔɔu yic:
“For Christ died for sins once for all, the righteous for the unrighteous, to bring you to God”. (1 Peter 3:18) “Luɔi cï Kritho käŋ guum aya natök në këde kërac, Raan piɛth piɔ̈u yen ajum käŋ në biäk de kɔc rac piɔɔth, luɔi bï yen wook lɛɛr të tɔ̈u ë Nhialic.” (I Peter 3:18)
“After his suffering, he showed himself to these men and gave many convincing proofs that he was alive. He appeared to them over a period of forty days and spoke about the kingdom of God.” (Acts 1:3) “Ɣɔn cï yen käŋ thöl ë guɔ̈m, kë jɔ rot nyuɔth keek kë pïir në kajuëe bïk ŋic ë gɔk. Aa cï tuɔ̈l tëde kek ë nïn kathiërŋuan, ku jɔ ya cool ë luel jam de kake cieŋ de Nhialic.” (Luɔɔide Tuc 1:3)
He Is the Only Way to God Yecu yen e Kueer Yetök:
"Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father but through Me.'" (John 14:6) (Yecu acï rot lɛ̈k kɔc) an, “Yɛn aye kueer, ku yɛn aye yic ku yɛn aye pïir, acïn raan bɔ̈ tëde Wä të kën yen tëëk tëde yɛn.” (Jɔn 14:6)
God has bridged this separation between us and Himself by sending His Son Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place. Nhialic acï Yecu tääu piny ke ye kueŋ cï thɔɔc në kaamde ke mɛnhde raan, në tuuc cïi yen wënde tuɔ̈ɔ̈c Yecu Kritho bï bɛ̈n thou në tim kɔ̈u në nyinda yic.
It is not enough just to know these three laws... Yiny de ŋuan bɛ̈, yen abï yïn tɔ̈ yök pïööc yic…

We must individually RECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

Suggestions for Christian GrowthBä Pïïrduɔ̈n de Wëi tɔ̈ Cil adï:
Spiritual growth results from trusting Jesus Christ. "...The righteous man shall live by faith" (Galatians 3:11). A life of faith will enable you to trust God increasingly with every detail of your life, and to practice the following: Ciɛl ee raan cil ku dïït ë kake wëiyiic e bɛ̈n ë ŋäth ee raan Yecu Kritho ŋɔ̈ɔ̈th. "Raan piɛthpiɔ̈u abï ya pïïr në gäm." (Galatai 3:11) Ee pïïr de gäm en bï yïn kuɔny bä Nhialic aŋɔ̈ɔ̈th ë këriëëcdu yic ëbɛ̈n. Ka bɔ̈ ë abïkï yïn kuɔny:
  • Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7)
  • Ye Nhialic lɔ̈ŋ ë ceŋ. (Jcn 15:7)
  • Read God's Word daily. (see Acts 17:11) Begin with the Gospel of John.
  • Ye wɛ̈t de Nhialic kueen ë ceŋ (Luɔɔi de Tuc 17:11) ku gɔl në Wëlpiɛth cï Jɔn ke gɔ̈ɔ̈r.
  • O bey God moment by moment. (John 14:21)
  • Ye Nhialic piŋ thok, ku gam thok në thaar thok ëbɛ̈n. (Jɔn 14:21)
  • Witness for Christ by your life and words. (see Matthew 4:19 and John 15:8)
  • Ye ya cäätɔ de Kritho në pïïrdu yic ku në jamdu yic. (Mathayo 4:19, Jcn 15:8)
  • T rust God for every detail of your life. (1 Peter 5:7)
  • Ye Nhialic ŋɔ̈ɔ̈th në kakuɔ̈n ke pïïr yic kedhie. (1 Peter 5:7)
  • H ave God control and empower your daily life and witness. (Galatians 5:16; Acts 1:8)
