People are Sinful MUNTU YE NDENGA MAMBO
"All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23) Mambo bonse balenga mambo kabiii bapelelelatu ne kufika ku lukamo Iwa Lesa ne. (Loma 3:23)

SUGGESTIONS FOR CHRISTIAN GROWTHBINTU BIMO BYAKONSHA KUKUKWASHA KUKU KOMESHA MU BWINA KILISHITU
Spiritual growth results from trusting Jesus Christ.Mwina Kilishitu usaka kukoma Ino milanguluko ikakukwasha:
  • Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7)
  • Kasa ulomaba kwi Lesa pajuba pajuba. (Yoano 15:7)
  • Read God's Word daily. (see Acts 17:11) Begin with the Gospel of John.
  • Kasa utanga bukumu wa Lesa pajuba pajuba. (Byubilo 17:11) Tendeka na buku wa Yoano.
  • Obey God moment by moment. (see John 14:21)
  • Kumakokela Lesa mu bimye byonse. (Yoano 14:21)
  • Witness for Christ by your life and words. (see Matthew 4:19 and John 15:8)
  • Kasa umutota Kilishitu mu bwikalo ne mu byambo. (Mateo 4:19; Yoano 15:8)
  • Trust God for every detail of your life (1 Peter 5:7).
  • Kasa upana milanguluko yobe kw Lesa. (I Petelo 5:7)
  • Have a regular meeting with others who are following Christ. (Hebrews 10:25)
  • Kasa upwila pamo na bena Kilishitu bakwenu. (Bahebelu 10:25)
FELLOWSHIP IN A GOOD CHURCHMWANEMENA LUMVWAÑANO LWA MWINA KILISHITU
The Bible tells us the importance of meeting together with other Christians (Hebrews 10:25).
Have you ever watched a fire burning in a fireplace? Several logs together burn brightly. But if you pull one log away from the fire, its flame soon goes out. The same thing happens to us if we do not spend time with other Christians. Attend a church where Christ is talked about and the Bible is taught. Start this week and make plans to attend regularly.		Buku witubula kupwila pamo no bakwetu bena Kilishitu. (Bahebalu 10:25) Bishiki bya mujilo inge biji pamo bitema bulongo, pakuba inge wafumyapo kimo ne kwikitula pa bunke bwakyo, kyeya kikepa, kabiji kiya na kuzhima. Wafwainwa kuya ku kipwilo ko bamunemeka Kilishitu ne byambo byanji, kasa bisapwilwa, kasa usambakana na bena Kilishitu bamwitaba’mba ye mupulushi wabo.
