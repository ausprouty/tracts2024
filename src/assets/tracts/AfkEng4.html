Have you heard of the Four Spiritual Laws?Het jy al gehoor van die Vier Geestelike Wette?
Just as there are physical laws that govern the physical universe, so are there spiritual laws that govern your relationship with God.Net soos daar natuurwette is wat die natuur beheer, is daar ook geestelike wette wat jou verhouding met God beheer.
1

God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

 1

God het jou LIEF en het `n wonderlike PLAN vir jou lewe.
God's Love GOD SE LIEFDE
“God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish, but have eternal life” (John 3:16 NIV). “God het die wêreld so liefgehad dat Hy Sy enigste Seun gegee het, sodat die wat in Hom glo, nie verlore sal gaan nie, maar die ewige lewe sal hê.” (Johannes 3:16)
God's Plan GOD SE PLAN
[Christ speaking] “I came that they might have life, and might have it abundantly” [that it might be full and meaningful] (John 10:10) [Jesus sê] “…Ek het gekom sodat hulle die lewe kan hê, en dit in oorvloed” [dat hulle lewe vol en betekenisvol kan wees] (Johannes 10: 10)
Why is it that most people are not experiencing the abundant life? Because... Waarom ervaar die meeste mense nie ‘n oorvloedige lewe nie?
Because… Omdat…
2

People are SINFUL and SEPARATED from God so we cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for our life.

 2

Die mens is SONDIG en van God GESKEI daarom kan hy nie God se liefde en plan vir sy lewe ken en ervaar nie.
People are Sinful DIE MENS IS SONDIG
"All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23) “Almal het gesondig en is ver van God af” (Romeine 3:23).
We were created to have a personal relationship with God, but by our own choice and self-will we have gone our own independent way and that relationship has been broken. This self-will, often seen as an attitude of active rebellion towards God or a lack of interest in Him, is an evidence of what the Bible calls sin. Die mens is geskape om ‘n verhouding met God te hê, maar kies om sy eie kop te volg en om onafhanklik van Hom te leef. Hierdie eiewilligheid wat lei tot opstandigheid teen God of ‘n algemene gebrek aan belangstelling in God, is `n bewys van wat die Bybel sonde noem.
People are Separated DIE MENS IS VAN GOD GESKEI
"The wages of sin is death" [spiritual separation from God]. (Romans 6:23) “Die loon wat die sonde gee, is die dood… ” (geestelike skeiding tussen God en mens). (Romeine 6:23).
This picture illustrates that God is holy and people are sinful. A great gap separates the two. The arrows illustrate that people are continually trying to reach God and the abundant life through their own efforts, such as a good life, philosophy, or religion – but they always fail. God is heilig en die mens is sondig. Sonde is soos ‘n diep kloof wat die twee skei. Die mens probeer gedurig om God deur sy eie pogings soos ‘n mooi lewe, goeie werke, filosofieë of godsdiens te bereik, maar al hierdie pogings is tevergeefs.
The third law explains the only way to bridge this gap… Die Derde Wet verduidelik hoe hierdie kloof oorbrug kan word…
3

Jesus Christ is God’s ONLY provision for our sin. Through Him alone you can know and experience God’s love and plan for your life.

 3

Jesus Christus is God se ENIGSTE oplossing vir die mens se sonde. Alleen deur Hom kan jy God se liefde en plan vir jou lewe ken en ervaar.
HE DIED IN OUR PLACE HY HET IN ONS PLEK GESTERF
"God demonstrates His own love toward us, in that while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us." (Romans 5:8) “…Maar God bewys Sy liefde vir ons juis hierin dat Christus vir ons gesterf het toe ons nog sondaars was.” (Romeine 5:8).
HE ROSE FROM THE DEAD DAARNA HET JESUS UIT DIE DOOD OPGESTAAN
"Christ died for our sins…He was buried…He was raised on the third day, according to the Scriptures…He appeared to Peter, then to the Twelve. After that He appeared to more than five hundred…" (1 Corinthians 15:3-6) “Na sy dood het Hy aan hulle met baie onbetwisbare bewyse ook getoon dat Hy lewe. In die loop van veertig dae het Hy by verskeie geleenthede aan hulle verskyn en met hulle oor die dinge van die koninkryk van God gepraat.” (Handelinge I :3)
He Is the Only Way to God HY IS DIE ENIGSTE WEG
"Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father but through Me.'" (John 14:6) “Jesus het vir hom gesê: ‘Ek is die weg en die waarheid en die lewe. Niemand kom na die Vader toe behalwe deur My nie.’” (Johannes 14:6).
This diagram illustrates that God has bridged the gulf that separates us from Him by sending His Son, Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place to pay the penalty for our sins. God het Sy Seun, Jesus Christus, na die wêreld gestuur om die k1oof van sonde wat ons van Hom skei te oorbrug. Hiervoor moes Jesus aan die kruis sterf om vir ons sondes te betaa1.
It is not enough just to know these three laws...
4

We must individually RECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

 4

Ons moet Jesus Christus persoonlik AANNEEM as Verlosser en Here. Slegs dan kan ons God se liefde en plan vir ons lewens ken en ervaar.
WE MUST RECEIVE CHRIST ONS MOET CHRISTUS AANNEEM
"As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, even to those who believe in His name." (John 1:12) “Maar aan almal wat Hom aangeneem het, dié wat in Hom glo, het Hy die reg gegee om kinders van God te word.” (Johannes 1:12)
We Receive Christ Through Faith ONS MOET CHRISTUS DEUR GELOOF AANNEEM
"By grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God; not as a result of works so that no one may boast." (Ephesians 2:8,9) “Julle is inderdaad uit genade gered, deur geloof. Hierdie redding kom nie uit julleself nie; dit is ‘n gawe van God. Dit kom nie deur julle eie verdienste nie, en daarom het niemand enige rede om op homself trots te wees nie” (Efesiërs 2:8,9).
WHEN YOU RECEIVE CHRIST, WE ARE BORN AGAIN WANNEER ONS CHRISTUS AANEEM, ERVAAR ONS WEDERGEBORE
When Christ comes into your life, you are born again. (Read John 3:18) Wanneer Christus in jou lewe inkom, word jy nuut gebore. (Lees Johannes 3: 18)
We Receive Christ by Personal Invitation ONS NEEM CHRISTUS AAN DEUR HOM IN ONS LEWE IN TE NOOI
[Christ speaking] "Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if anyone hears My voice and opens the door, I will come in to him." (Revelation 3:20) [Christus sê]: "Kyk, Ek staan by die deur en Ek klop. As iemand my stem hoor en die deur oopmaak, sal Ek by hom ingaan…" (Openbaring 3:20).
Receiving Christ involves turning to God from self (repentance) and trusting Christ to come into our lives to forgive our sins and to make us what He wants us to be. Just to agree intellectually that Jesus Christ is the Son of God and that He died on the cross for our sins is not enough. Nor is it enough to have an emotional experience. We receive Jesus Christ by faith, as an act of the will. Wanneer jy Christus aanneem, kies jy orn jou eiewilligheid te los en God se wil te doen (bekering). Jy glo dat Christus in jou lewe inkom, jou sondes vergewe en jou verander soos Hy wil.
Just to agree intellectually that Jesus Christ is the Son of God and that He died on the cross for our sins is not enough. Nor is it enough to have an emotional experience. We receive Jesus Christ by faith, as an act of the will. 0m net te weet dat Jesus Christus die Seun van God is en dat Hy aan die kruis vir ons sondes gesterf het, is nie genoeg nie. Dit is ook nie genoeg om ‘n emosionele ervaring te hê nie. Jy neem Christus deur geloof aan. Dit is ‘n wilsbesluit.

Did you receive Christ into your life? According to His promise in Revelation 3:20, where is Christ right now in relation to you? Christ said that He would come into your life. Would He mislead you? On what authority do you know that God has answered your prayer? (The trustworthiness of God Himself and His Word.)Het jy die Here Jesus in jou lewe aangeneen? In die lig van Openbaring 3 :20, waar is Christus op hierdie oomblik met betrekking tot jouself? Christus het gesê Hy sal in jou lewe inkom. Sal Hy jou mislei? Op grond waarvan weet jy dat God jou gebed verhoor het? (Die betroubaarheid van God Self en die Bybel.)
The Bible promises Eternal Life to all who receive Christ.DIE BYBEL BELOWE DIE EWIGE LEWE AAN ELKEEN WAT CHRISTUS AANNEEM
"God has given us eternal life, and this life is in His Son. He who has the Son has the life; he who does not have the Son of God does not have the life. These things I have written to you who believe in the name of the Son of God, so that you may know that you have eternal life." (1 John 5:11-13)“En die getuienis behels dit: God het ons die ewige lewe gegee, en dié lewe is deur sy Seun. Wie die Seun het, het die lewe; wie nie die Seun van God het nie, het ook nie die lewe nie. Thank God often that Christ is in your life and that He will never leave you (Hebrews 13:5). He will not deceive you.Dank God gereeld dat Christus in jou lewe is en dat Hy jou nooit sal verlaat of in die steek sal laat nie (Hebreërs 13:5). Jy kan weet dat die lewende Christus in jou woon en dat jy die ewige lewe het vanaf die oomblik dat jy Hom op grond van sy belofte ingenooi het. Hy sal jou nie bedrieg nie.

Do Not rely on your FeelingsMOENIE OP GEVOEL STAATMAAK NIE
The promise of God's Word, the Bible - not our feelings - is our authority. The Christian lives by faith (trust) in the trustworthiness of God Himself and His Word. This train diagram illustrates the relationship among fact (God and His Word), faith (our trust in God and His Word), and feeling (the result of our faith and obedience). (Read John 14:21)Ons maak staat op die beloftes van die Bybel (God se Woord) en nie op ons gevoel nie. Die Christen plaas sy geloof in God en Sy Woord. Die skets hieronder illustreer die verband tussen Waarheid (God en Sy Woord), Geloof (ons vertroue in God en Sy Woord) en Gevoel (die gevolg van ons geloof en gehoorsaamheid). (Johannes 14:21)
The train can travel with or without the guard's van. however the guard's van cannot pull the train. In the same way Christians do not rely on feeling and emotion. We place our trust in God and his promises because He can be trusted.Die trein kan met of sonder die kondukteurswa ry. Dit sou egter tevergeefs wees om die trein deur die kondukteurswa te probeer trek. Op dieselfde wyse maak ons as Christene nie op gevoelens en emosies staat nie, maar ons vestig ons geloof (vertroue) op die betroubaarheid van God en die beloftes van sy Woord.

HINTS FOR SPIRITUAL GROWTHWENKE VIR GEESTELIKE GROEI
The more you trust Jesus Christ with every aspect of your life, the more you will grow spiritually. “The righteous shall live by faith.” (Galatians 2:1)Hoe meer jy Jesus Christus met elke aspek van jou lewe vertrou, hoe meer sal jy geestelik groei. "Die regverdige sal uit die geloof lewe". (Galasiërs 2:1 ).
  • Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7)
  • Bid. Praat daagliks met God. (Johannes 15:7).
  • Read God’s Word daily (2 Timothy 3:16, Acts 17:11); begin with the Gospel of John.
  • Lees die Bybel elke dag. (2 Timoteus 3 : 16; Handelinge I7:11) begin met die Evangelie volgens Johannes.
  • Obey God moment by moment. (see John 14:21)
  • Gehoorsaam. Doen wat God jou leer in Sy Woord (Johannes 14:21).
  • Witness for Christ by your life and words. (see Matthew 4:19 and John 15:8)
  • Getuig. Laat jou woorde en lewe getuig van wat Christus vir jou doen. (Matteus 4: 19; Johannes 15:8).
  • Trust God for every detail of your life (1 Peter 5:7).
  • Vertrou. Vertrou God met elke aspek van jou lewe. (1 Petrus 5:7).
  • Holy Spirit – allow Him to control and empower your daily life and witness (Galatians 5:16,17; Acts 1:8).
  • Onderwerp. Stel jou onder beheer van die Heilige Gees. Laat Hy jou bekragtig om die Christelike lewe te lei. (Galasiërs 5:16,17; Handelinge 1:8).
FELLOWSHIP IN A GOOD CHURCHCHRISTELIKE SAMESYN IN ‘N GOEIE KERK
The Bible tells us the importance of meeting together with other Christians (Hebrews 10:25).Have you ever watched a fire burning in a fireplace? Several logs together burn brightly. But if you pull one log away from the fire, its flame soon goes out. The same thing happens to us if we do not spend time with other Christians. Attend a church where Christ is talked about and the Bible is taught. Start this week and make plans to attend regularly.Hebreers 10:25 waarsku: “Ons moenie van die samekomste van die gemeente af wegbly soos party se gewoonte is nie, maar mekaar eerder aanmoedig om daarheen te gaan…:” Baie hout wat saam brand, maak ’n groot vuur. Wanneer een stomp uit die vuur gehaal en eenkant neergesit word, gaan dit gou dood. So is dit ook met jou verhouding met ander Christene. As jy nie aan ’n kerk behoort nie, moenie wag om uitgenooi te word nie. Neem die inisiatief, bel die dominee van ’n plaaslike kerk waar God aanbid word en sy Woord gepreek word. Begin reeds hierde week en beplan om gereeld kerk toe te gaan.
Make friends with people who also know the Lord Jesus who bear the fruit of the Spirit.(Galatians 5:22)Maak vriende met mense wat ook die Here Jesus persoonlik ken en die vrug van die Heilige Gees dra. (Galasiërs 5:22).
Go and tell someone else about your meeting with Jesus.Gaan vertel iemand anders van jou ontmoeting met Jesus
