Have You Heard of the Four Spiritual Laws?Bushe walishiba ifintu fine ifingalosho muntu ku mweo wine wine?
There are natural laws that control the world in which we live. There are also spiritual laws which determine your relationship with God.Pano isonde apo twikala ifintu fyonse fyendela umwalola umafunde ayo Lesa afipagile, Ni fyo fine mu fintu fya kwe Lesa mwaliba amafunde ya kututeka.
1

Law One

God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

 1

IF UNDE LYA KUBALILAPO.

LESA ALIKUTEMWA, ALEKUTAMBIKE NSHILA IYAWAMISHA IYO AKUTEYANISHISHA.
GOD’S LOVE UKUTEMWA KWA KWA LESA.
“God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish, but have eternal life” (John 3:16 NIV). Pantu Lesa atemenwe aba pano isonde ukutemwa kwa kuti apele Umwana wakwe uwafyalwa eka, ukuti onse uumutetekela eonaika, lelo abe no mweo wa muyayaya. Johane 3:16
GOD’S PLAN UBUTEYANYO BWA KWA LESA.
[Christ speaking] “I came that they might have life, and might have it abundantly” (John 10:10). Kristu atile: "Ine ninjisa ukuti babe no mweo, umweo wine uwacilamo." Yohane 10:10
Why aren’t all people experiencing the abundant life? Because… No mba te bonse baishibo mweo uno uwine wine. Wala mona icalenga.
2

Law Two

Man is SINFUL and SEPARATED from God. Thus, he cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for his life.]

 2

IFUNDE LYA BUBILI.

ABANTU NI BAMPULAMAFUNDE, ABAPULUMUSHI, KABILI BALIPATUKA KULI LESA. E ICO BAFILWO KWISHIBA INSHILA IYO LESA ABATEYANISHISHA.
MAN IS SINFUL ABANTU BALI NE MIBELE YA BUBIFI.
“All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God” (Romans 3:23). Bonse nababifya, kabili bapelebela ku bukata bwa kwa lesa. Roma 3:23
Man was created to have fellowship with God; but, because of his stubborn self-will, he chose to go his own independent way, and fellowship with God was broken. This self-will, characterized by an attitude of active rebellion or passive indifference, is an evidence of what the Bible calls sin. Lesa abumbile abantu ukuti bomfwane nankwe bwino bwino. Lelo basalile ukuyendela fye, na bucibusa bwaliputwike palya pene. Baibele ati ici cintu e bupulumushi. Cailanga (1) ilyo abantu basangukila Lesa, (2) ilyo tabasakamana Lesa nangu kuposapo amano, (3) ilyo mu mikalile yabo tabekala ukulingana ne cipimo ca kwa Lesa.
MAN IS SEPARATED ABANTU BALIPATUKA KULI LESA.
“The wages of sin is death” [spiritual separation from God] (Romans 6:23). Icilambu ca bupulumushi ni mfwa (e kutila ukupatuka na Lesa). Roma 6:23
God is holy and man is sinful. There is a great separation between the two. The arrows show how man tries to reach God and the abundant life through his own efforts: such as living a good life, being religious, helping others etc., but all his efforts fail. Lesa wa mushilo; abantu balibipa. Bali-talukana sana na Lesa. Imifwi twashila apa yatulanga ifyo abantu besho kufika kuli Lesa mu maka yabo abene kabili mu misango iyalekanalekana, awe yonse yaleuka fye.
The third law gives the only answer to this problem… If unde lya butatu e lingatupikuwilo bwile ubu. Walalimona.
3

Law Three

Jesus Christ is God’s ONLY provision for man’s sin. Through Him you can know and experience God’s love and plan for your life.

 3

IFUNDE LYA BUTATU.

YESU KRISTU E NSHILA IMO MPO IYA KUFIKILA KULI LESA. ALITUFWILILE. MULI WENE E MO WINGAMWISHIBILA NO KUTEMWA KWAKWE, NO KWISHIBE NSHILA LESA AKUTEYANISHISHA.
HE DIED IN OUR PLACE ALIFWILE NO KWIMA KABILI KU BAFWA.
“For Christ also suffered once for sins, the righteous for the unrighteous, to bring you to God. He was put to death in the body but made alive in the Spirit." (1 Peter 3:18) Pa kuti Kristu aculile aimbifyo umuku umo, uwalungama aculile pa mulandu wa bashalungama, ukuti atupalamike kuli Lesa. 1 Petro 3:18
“After his suffering, he presented himself to them and gave many convincing proofs that he was alive. He appeared to them over a period of forty days and spoke about the kingdom of God.” (Acts 1:3). Ailangile uwa mweo mu fishibilo ifingi, alemoneka kuli bene mu nshiku amakumi yane, no kusose fya bufumu bwa kwa Lesa. Imilimo 1:3
HE IS THE ONLY WAY YESU E NSHILA IMO MPO.
“Jesus said to him, ‘I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father, but through Me.’” (John 14:6) Yesu atile, "Ni ne nshila ne cine no mweo; takuli uwisa kuli Tata, kano aishila muli ine." Yohane 14:6
God has bridged this separation between us and Himself by sending His Son Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place. Lesa alipango bulalo ubwa kuti twabukile kuli wene, no bulalo ubu ni Yesu umwine. Lesa atumine Umwana wa kwe Yesu Kristu ku kutufwila pa cimuti capindama.
It is not enough just to know these three laws … Tacilingile ukwishiba fye aya mafunde yatatu, kano ukucite cintu walabelonga nomba.
4

Law Four

We must individually RECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

 4

IFUNDE LYA BUNE.

BONSE TULI NO KUPOKELELA YESU KRISTU NGO MUPUSUSHI WESU KABILI SHIKULU WA KUTUTEKA.LYENA KUTI TWAISHIBA UKUTEMWA KWA KWA LESA NE NSHILA ATUTEYANISHISHA.
WE MUST RECEIVE CHRIST TULI NO KUPOKELELA KRISTU MU CITETEKELO.
“As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, even to those who believe in His name” (John 1:12) Abo bene abamusekelele, abatetekele ishina lyakwe, e bo apêle amaka aya kuba abana ba kwa Lesa. (Yohane 1:12)
“By grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God; not as a result of works, that no one should boast” (Ephesians 2:8,9). Ni ku kusenamina mwapusukila, pa kutetekela; kabili te cintu cafuma kuli imwe, ca bupe ca kwa Lesa; tacafuma mu milimo, pa kuti umuntu tengatakisha. Efese 2:8, 9
WE RECEIVE CHRIST THROUGH PERSONAL INVITATION NI MU KWITA KRISTU ENGILE MULI IFWE KU KUTUTEKA E MWALOLA UKUPOKELELA KRISTU.
[Christ speaking] “Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if any one hears My voice and opens the door, I will come in to him.” (Revelation 3:20) Kristu atile. "Mona, ninjiminina pa mwinshi no kukunkunsha; umuntu nga aumfwe shiwi lyandi no kwisulako ku mwinshi, ndeingila kuli wene." Ukusokolola 3:20
Receiving Christ involves turning to God from self (repentance) and trusting Christ to come into our lives to forgive our sins and to make us what He wants us to be. Just to agree intellectually that Jesus Christ is the Son of God and that He died on the cross for our sins is not enough. Nor is it enough to have an emotional experience. We receive Jesus Christ by faith, as an act of the will. Tacilingile ukusumina fye ukutila kwena amafundisho ya kwa Kristu ya cine cine iyo. Spiritual growth results from trusting Jesus Christ.Nomba uli mwina Kristu, ufwile ukulakula. Ifintu ifilekonkana pano kuti fyakwafwa:
1. Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7).1. Ulepepa umo shacela (Yohane 15:7).
2. Read God’s Word daily (Acts 17:11); begin with the Gospel of John.2. Ulebelenga mwi buku lya kwa Lesa inshiku shonse (Imilimo 17:11).
3. Obey God moment by moment (John 14:21).3. Cawama ukutampa mu Mbila Nsuma ya kwa Yohane. Ulenakila Lesa lyonse fye (Yohane 14:21).
4. Witness for Christ by your life and words (Matthew 4:19; John 15:8).4. Mu mikalile yobe na mu kulanda kobe ulebe nte ya kwa Kristu (Mateo 4:19. Yohane 15:8).
5. Trust God for every detail of your life (1 Peter 5:7).5. Uletetekeia Lesa ukuti akwafwe muli tonse (I Petro 5:7).
6. Have God control and empower your daily life and witness (Galatians 5:16 & Acts 1:8).6. Leka Lesa akuteke no kukupêla amaka mu milimo yobe iya kumubombela inshiku shonse (Galatia 5:16, 17. lmilimo 1:8).
