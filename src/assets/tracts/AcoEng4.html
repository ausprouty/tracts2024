Have you heard of the Four Spiritual Laws?IN MONO IWINYO PI CIK ANGWEN ME CWINY?
There are natural laws that control the world in which we live. There are also spiritual laws which determine your relationship with God.Tye cik me lobo ma gwoko lobo maa ma makwo lye ni. Dok bene tye cik me cwinyma tye ka notte ikin in ki Lubanga meri.
1

LAW ONE

God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

 1

Cik me Acel

Lubanga omaro in dok bene en tye ki yub maber oi kwo ma meri.
God's Love Mar pa Lubanga
“God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish, but have eternal life.” (John 3:16) Pien Lubanga con omaro lobo kumeno, omiyo iye Wode acel keken, wek dano ducu ma ye en owek to, ento obed ki kwo ma pe tum.Jon 3:16
God's Plan Yub pa Lubanga
[Christ speaking] “I came that they might have life, and might have it abundantly” [that it might be full and meaningful]. (John 10:10) (Rwot Yecu owaco,)”An abino wek gunong kwo, gubed kwede madwong.”Jon 10:10
2

LAW TWO

People are SINFUL and SEPARATED from God so we cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for our life.

 2

Cik me Aryo

Dano en labal dik opoke woko ki Lubanga, pi man en pe twero ngeyo dok winyo mar pa Lubanga ki yub pi kwone.
People are Sinful Dano en labal
"All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23) Pien dano ducu gubak\lo pe gio I kom deyo pa Li\ubanga. Jo Roma 3:23
We were created to be friends with God; but we chose to go our own way, and friendship with God was broken. This is what the Bible calls sin. This is shown: Dano gicweyo me bedo larerm ki Lubanga. Ento en okwanyo yo ma mere cibedo larem ki Lubanga otur woko, Man aye gin ma Jiri lwongo ni bal. Man nyute
when people rebel against God, Ikare ma dano ojemo ikom Lubanga
when people do not care about God and Ikare ma en pe tamo pi Lubanga, ki
when people do not live up to God's perfect standard Ka en pe kwo ma pe atir inyim Lubanga
People are Separated Dano Opoke Woko
"The wages of sin is death" [spiritual separation from God]. (Romans 6:23) Pien mucara me bal en to (poke i cwiny ki Lubanga). Jo Roma 6:23
God is holy and people are sinful. There is a great separation between the two. The arrows show how people try to reach God and the abundant life through their own efforts: such as living a good life, being religious, helping others etc., but all their efforts fail. Lubanga pe ki bal ento dano lubalo. Tye apokapoka madit ikin jo aryo man. Lacim megu nyuto kit ma dano tye ka teme kwede me oo bot Lubanga, ki me nongo kwo ma pe tum, ki teko ma mere: kwo ki kwo maber, bedo ladini, konyo dano mukene, ento tutine lal nono.
The third law gives the only answer to this problem… Cik me adek miyo botwa lagam ma cobo peko man…
3

LAW THREE

Jesus Christ is God’s ONLY provision for our sin. Through Him alone you can know and experience God’s love and plan for your life.

 3

Cik me Adek

Yecu Kricito aye yo ma dano oo kwede bot Luban-ga. En oto pi wan. Ki bot Yecu iromo ngeyo Lubanga dok iwinyo mar mere kacel ki yub ma en tye kwede pi kwo ma meri.
He Died and Rose From the Dead En oto ka ocer ki I liyel.
“For Christ died for sins once for all, the righteous for the unrighteous, to bring you to God”. (1Peter 3:18) Pien Kricito bene yam onene ca pi bal wang, kicel ma otyeko ducu: en ngat ma kite atir oto po jo ma kitgi pe atir, wek omi ekelwa bot Lubanga…1 Petero 3:18
“After his suffering, he showed himself to these men and gave many convincing proofs that he was alive. He appeared to them over a period of forty days and spoke about the kingdom of God.” (Acts 1:3) Obedo ka nen botgi tyen mapol pi moko ni etye makwo lacen ki I nge tone. Obedo ka nen botgi pi nino pyerangwen (Tic pa Lukwena 1:3)
He Is the Only Way to God En keken aye yo
"Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father but through Me.'" (John 14:6) Yecu odokke iye ni, “An a yo, ki ada, ki kwo: Pe tye ngati mo ma bino bot Won, kono ki I en keken”Jon 14:6.
God has bridged this separation between us and Himself by sending His Son Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place. Lubanga omwona woko apokapoka man ma tye I kin wa ki En ki cwalo Wode Yecu Kricito me too I kon yatarita u kakarwa.
It is not enough just to know these three laws... Ngeyo cik adek man pe twero konyo wa …

LAW FOUR

We must individually RECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

 4

Cik me Angwen

We Receive Christ Through Faith Myero wajol Kricito
"As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, even to those who believe in His name." (John 1:12) Ento dano ducu ma ceng gujole, ma guye nyinge, omiyogi twero me doko litino pa Lubanga. Jon 1:12
"By grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God; not as a result of works so that no one may boast." (Ephesians 2:8,9) Pien pi kicane omiyo en olarowu pi niye. Man pe otimme pi tic mo ma win wutiyo; meno obedo mot pa Lubanga gire. Pe otimme pi ticwi mo, mumiyo ngat mo owek wakke pire. Jo Epeco 2:8-9.
We Receive Christ by inviting him into our lives Watwero jolo Kricito ki lwongo En i kwowa
It is not enough just to agree with Christ’s teachings. Receiving Christ means turning to God from self (repenting). We trust Christ to come into our lives and to forgive us our sins. We let Him make us what he wants us to be. Pe romo me ye pwony pa Kricito keken. Jolo Kricito keken. Joko Kricito nyutu lokke bot Lubanga ki gin abalawa (tucu bal). Wageno Kricito me bino i kwowa ki me bwoto gin abalawa. Wawek En olok wa kit ma En mito wabed kwede.

Suggestions for Christian GrowthKit me dongo me Cwiny
As a Christian we should grow. This outline will help you:Macalo lakricitayo, mito i dong. Tam magu twero konyo in.
  • Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7)
  • Citi bot Lubanga ki lega nino ducu. Jon 15:7
  • Read God's Word daily. (see Acts 17:11) Begin with the Gospel of John.
  • Kwan lok pa Lubanga nino ducu. Tic pa Lukwena 17:11. Cak ki Jiri pa Jon.
  • Obey God moment by moment. (John 14:21)
  • Winy cik pa Lubanga, kare ki kare. Jon 14:21
  • Witness for Christ by your life and words. (see Matthew 4:19 and John 15:8)
  • Bed lacaden pa Kricito ki kwo wa ki lok ma meri. Matayo 4:19; Jon 15:8
  • Trust God for every detail of your life. (1 Peter 5:7)
  • Gen Lubanga i kwo ni ducu. 1Peter 5:7
  • Have God control and empower your daily life and witness. (Galatians 5:16 & Acts 1:8)
  • Bed ki teko pa Lubanga, wek olo kwoni nino ducu ki caden. Jo Galatiya 5:16,17 ki Tic pa Lukwena 1:8
