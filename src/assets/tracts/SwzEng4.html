Have you heard of the Four Spiritual Laws? WASE WEVA YINI NGEMAQINISO LAMANE AKAMOYA NA?
1

God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

1

Nkulunkulu UYAKUTSANDZA futsi ukubekele LIPULANI lelimangalisako ngemphilo yakho.

God's Love LUTSANDVO LWANKULUNKULU
“God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish, but have eternal life” (John 3:16 NIV). Nkulunkulu walitsandza live kakhulu kangangekutsi wadzimate wanikela ngeNdvodzana yakhe lekukuphela kwayo, kute kutsi nome ngubani lokholwa ngiyo angabhubhi, kodvwa abe nekuphila lokuphakadze. (Johane 3:16)
God's Plan LIPULANI LANKULUNKULU
Jesus said, "I came that they may have life, and have it abundantly" [that it might be full and meaningful]." (John 10:10) Mine ngitele kwehutsi bantfu babe nekuphila, babe nako kuphuphume. (Johane 10:10).
Not all people are not experiencing the abundant life because.... Akusibo bonkhe labayiphilako lemphilo lephuphumako ngobe...
2

People are SINFUL and SEPARATED from God so we cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for our life.

2

Umuntfu WONILE futsi WEHLUKENE nankulunkulu, ngakoke Akalwati lutsandvo lwankulunkulu nelipulani lakhe Ngemphilo yakhe.

People are Sinful UMUNTFU WONILE
"All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23) Bonkhe bantfu bonile, bakhashane kakhulu nenkhatimulo yaNkulunkulu. (Roma 3:23)
We were created to have a personal relationship with God, but by our own choice and self-will we have gone our own independent way and that relationship has been broken. This self-will, often seen as an attitude of active rebellion towards God or a lack of interest in Him, is an evidence of what the Bible calls sin. Umuntfu bekadalelwe kutsi abe ngumngani waNkulunkulu, kodvwa umuntfu wakhetsa kuhamba ngeyakhe indlela, ngako-ke libhayi-bheli likubita ngekutsi sono
People are Separated UMUNTFU WEHLUKENE NANKULUNKULU
"The wages of sin is death" [spiritual separation from God]. (Romans 6:23) Inkhokhelo yesono kufa; kodvwa sipho semusa saNkulunkulu sikuphila lokuphakadze ngaKristu Jesu; Inkhosi yetfu. (Roma 6:23)
God is holy and man is sinful. There is a great gulf between the two. Man tries to reach God through his own efforts, living a good life, being religious, helping others, etc., but all his efforts fail. Nkulunkulu ungcwele, umuntfu usoni. Kunemkhatsi lomkhulu phakatsi kwaNkulunkulu nemuntfu. Umuntfu utama ngayo yonkhe indlela kususa lomkhatsi kute bahlangane naNkulunkulu. Utama kuphila imphilo lenhle ngekuya esontfweni, kusita labahluphekako, kodvwa konkhe loku akusiti ngalutfo.
The third law explains the only way to bridge this gulf… Liqiniso lesitsatfu ngilo lodvwa lelisinika impenavulo kulenkinga
3

Jesus Christ is God’s ONLY provision for our sin. Through Him alone you can know and experience God’s love and plan for your life.

3

Jesu Kristu NGUYE yedvwa indlela lesingafika ngayo Kunkulunkulu. Yena wasifela. Ngaye ungamati nkulunkulu Nelutsandvo lwakhe, kanye nelipulani lakhe ngekuphila Kwakho.

HE DIED AND ROSE FROM THE DEAD WAFA WAPHINDZE WAVUKA KULABAFILE
“After his suffering, he showed himself to these men and gave many convincing proofs that he was alive. He appeared to them over a period of forty days and spoke about the kingdom of God.” (Acts 1:3) Emvakwekuhlupheka kwakhe kwaphela emalanga langemashumi lamane avukile ekufeni. Wativeta kubo ngetindlela letinyenti letaticinisa ngalokungangabateki kutsi uyaphila; wabonwa ngabo akhuluma kubo ngembuso waNkulunkulu. (Imisebenti yebaPhostoli 1:3)
He Is the Only Way to God NGUYE YEDVWA LOYINDLELA
"Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father but through Me.'" (John 14:6) Jesu watsi kuye: “Mine ngiyindlela, neliciniso, nekuphila. Kute longeta kuBabe nakangeti ngami.” (Johane 14:6)
This diagram illustrates that God has bridged the gulf that separates us from Him by sending His Son, Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place to pay the penalty for our sins. Nkulunkulu usivalile lesikhala lesihlukanisa tsine naye, ngekutfumela iNdvodzana yakhe Jesu Kristu, kutsi itekusifela esiphambanweni.
It is not enough just to know these three laws... Akukeneli kwati lamaciniso lamatsatfu kuphela

We must individually RECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

4

Sifanele kutsi kube nguloyo naloyo AMEMUKELE Jesu Kristu abe Yinkhosi nemsindzisi wakhe kutsi silwati lutsandvo Lwankulunkulu nelipulani lakhe ngekuphila kwetfu.

WE MUST RECEIVE CHRIST NGEKUKHOLWA
"As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, even to those who believe in His name." (John 1:12) Kepha bonkhe labamemukela, ngekukholwa egameni lakhe, wabapha emandla ekutsi babe bantfwana baNkulunkulu. (Johane 1:12)
"By grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God; not as a result of works so that no one may boast." (Ephesians 2:8,9) Empeleni kusindziswa kwenu ngekukholwa kuya ngemusa, futsi loko akuveli kini, sipho nje saNkulunkulu. Loko akuveli ngemisebenti, funa babekhona labatibongako. (Basefesu 2:8,9)

You Can Receive Christ Right Now by Faith Through Prayer UNGABMEMUKELA KRISTU KHONA NYALO
God knows your heart and is not so concerned with your words as He is with the attitude of your heart. The following is a suggested prayer: Mcele kutsi akwentele loko lekakutsembisile. Yena unaka kakhulu lokufiswa yinhlitiyo yakho kunalokushoko ngemlomo wakho ungakhuleka ngalendlela lelandzelako.
Lord Jesus, I need You. Thank you for dying on the cross for my sins. I open the door of my life and receive You as my Savior and Lord. Thank You for forgiving my sins and giving me eternal life. Take control of the throne of my life. Make me the kind of person You want me to be. “Nkhosi Jesu, ngiyakudzinga. Kadze ngitiphatsele mine imphilo yami, ngaloko ngonile kuwe. Ngiyabonga kutsi ungitsetselele tono tami. Ngena emphilweni yami uyibuse. Ngente ngibe nguloko lotsandza kutsi ngibe nguko.”
Does this prayer express the desire of your heart? If it does, I invite you to pray this prayer right now. Ngabe lomthandazo usho kona yini loko, lokufiswa yinhlitiyo yakho?
Christ will come into your life as He promised. Nangabe usho kona, wuthandaze nyalo, Kristu utawungena emphilweni yakho njengekwetsemba kwakho.
HOW TO KNOW THAT CHRIST IS IN YOUR LIFE UNGATI KANJANI KUTSI KRISTU SEWUSEMPHILWENI
Did you receive Christ into your life? According to His promise in Revelation 3:20, where is Christ right now in relation to you? Christ said that He would come into your life. Would He mislead you? On what authority do you know that God has answered your prayer? (The trustworthiness of God Himself and His Word.) Sewumemukele yini Kristu kutsi angene emphilweni yakho na? Ngekwesetsembiso sakhe lesitfolakala kuSembulo 3:20, ukuphi Kristu? Utsite utawungena emphilweni yakho. Angakuyenga yini? Wati ngani kutsi Nkulunkulu sewuwuphendvulile umthandazo wakho? (ngekwetsemba Nkulunkulu neLivi lakhe)
THE BIBLE PROMISES ETERNAL LIFE (1 JOHN 5:11–13) LIBHAYIBHELI LIBATSEMBISA KUPHILA LOKUPHAKADZE BONKHE LABAMEMUKELAKO KRISTU (I JOHANE 5:11-13)
Thank God often that Christ is in your life and that He will never leave you (Hebrews 13:5). You can know on the basis of His promise that Christ lives in you and that you have eternal life from the very moment you invite Him in. He will not deceive you. Mbonge Nkulunkulu ngetikhatsi tonkhe ngekutsi Kristu usemphilweni yakho. Ushito kutsi angeke akushiye nanini (Emahebheru 13:5). Ungati kutsi Kristu usemphilweni yakho nekutsi unekuphila lokuphakadze ngenca yesitsembiso sakhe.
An important reminder.. Sitawutsini ngemizwa
Do Not rely on your Feelings UNGAHAMBI NGEMIZWA
The Christian lives by believing God and what He says in the Bible. The promise of God's Word is our authority. This drawing shows how the Truth of the Bible, Faith and Feelings work together in your Christian life. UmKristu uphila ngekukholwa Nkulunkulu naloko lakushoko eBhayibhelini. Setsembiso saNkulunkulu ngusona siligunya letfu. Lomfanekiso wemoto ukhombisa liciniso lelibhayibheli, kukholwa nemizwa kutsi kusebenta kanye emphilweni yakho yebuKristu.
  • Truth has the power to lead us.
  • Liciniso linemandla ekusihola.
  • We are joined to the power of Truth by Faith
  • Sihlanganiswa nemandla eliciniso ngekukholwa.
  • As we obey the Truth, our feelings follow along in the right way.
  • Sisalalela liciniso, imizwa yetfu ilandzela ngendlela lengiyona yona.
  • We trust God and His Truth, not our Feelings
  • Setsemba Nkulunkulu neliciniso lakhe, hhayi imizwa yetfu.
NOW THAT YOU HAVE RECEIVED CHRIST NYALO LOSEWUMEMUKELE KRISTU
The moment you received Christ by faith, as an act of the will, many things happened, including the following: Ngesikhatsi umamukela Kristu tinyenti tintfo letentekako:
  • Christ came into your life (Revelation 3:20 and Colossians 1:27)
  • Kristu ungena emphilweni yakho (Sembulo 3:20, KubaseKholose 1:27)
  • Your sins were forgiven (Colossians 1:14)
  • Tono takho titsetselelwe (KubaseKholose 1:14)
  • You became a child of God (John 1:12)
  • Sewungumtfwana waNkulunkuiu (Johane 1:12)
  • You began the great adventure for which God created you (John 10:10)
  • Sewutawucala kulati lipulani laNkulunkulu nenhloso yakhe ngemphilo yakho (Johane 10:10)
Can you think of anything more wonderful that could happen to you than receiving Christ? Would you like to thank God in prayer right now for what He has done for you? By thanking God, you demonstrate your faith. Ucabanga kutsi ikhona yini intfo lengenteka lencono kunekwemukela Kristu? Ungatsandza kubonga Nkulunkulu ngemthandazo nyalo ngaloko lakwentele kona? Kubonga Nkulunkulu kukhombisa kukholwa kwakho Asikhuleke.
SUGGESTIONS FOR CHRISTIAN GROWTH UNGAKHULA KANJANI EMOYENI
Spiritual growth results from trusting Jesus Christ. "...The righteous man shall live by faith" (Galatians 3:11). A life of faith will enable you to trust God increasingly with every detail of your life, and to practice the following: NjengemKristu kudzingeka kutsi ukhule. Letincomo letilandzelako tingakusita.
  • Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7)
  • Thandaza kuNkulunkulu onkhe emalanga (Johane 15:7)
  • Read God's Word daily. (see Acts 17:11) Begin with the Gospel of John.
  • Fundza livi laNkulunkulu onkhe emalanga (Tento 17:11 )
  • Obey God moment by moment. (see John 14:21)
  • Fakazela Kristu ngemphilo nangalokukhulumako (Matewu 4:19 na Johane 15:8)
  • Trust God for every detail of your life (1 Peter 5:7).
  • Tsemba Nkulunkulu kuyo yonkhe intfo lecondzene nekuphila kwakho (1 Phetro 5:7)
  • Holy Spirit – allow Him to control and empower your daily life and witness (Galatians 5:16,17; Acts 1:8).
  • Vumela Nkulunkulu kutsi akuhole aphindze akunike emandla ekuphila ngekufakaza kwakho (BaseGalathiya 5:16,17 Imisebenti yabaPhostoli 1:8)
FELLOWSHIP IN A GOOD CHURCH LIBANDLA LIBALULEKILE
The Bible tells us the importance of meeting together with other Christians (Hebrews 10:25).
Have you ever watched a fire burning in a fireplace? Several logs together burn brightly. But if you pull one log away from the fire, its flame soon goes out. The same thing happens to us if we do not spend time with other Christians. Attend a church where Christ is talked about and the Bible is taught. Start this week and make plans to attend regularly.
