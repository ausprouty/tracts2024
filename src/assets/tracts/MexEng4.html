2

LAW TWO

People are SINFUL and SEPARATED from God so we cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for our life.

 2

FAHAROA

Mpanota ny olombelona ka tafasaraka amin’Andriamanitra, noho izany dia tsy fantany ny fitiavan’Andriamanitra sy ny fiainana atolotr’Andriamanitra azy.
People are Sinful MPANOTA NY OLOMBELONA.
"All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23) “Fa samy efa nanota izy rehetra ka tsy manana ny voninahitra avy amin’Andriamanitra“. (Romana 3:23)
We were created to have a personal relationship with God, but by our own choice and self-will we have gone our own independent way and that relationship has been broken. This self-will, often seen as an attitude of active rebellion towards God or a lack of interest in Him, is an evidence of what the Bible calls sin. Noforonin’Andriamanitra mba hanana fifandraisana mitohy Aminy ny olombelona. Nifidy ny handeha araka ny sitrapony anefa izy; vokatr’izany dia rava ny fifandraisany amin’Andriamanitra. Na ny fanoherana mivantana an’Andriamanitra, na ny tsy firehana manoloana ny Tenin’Andriamanitra dia samy antsoin’ny Baiboly hoe “fahotana“ avokoa.
People are Separated TAFASARAKA AMIN’ANDRIAMANITRA NY OLOMBELONA
“Your iniquities have separated you from God…
(Isaiah 59:2a) 		“Fa ny helokareo no efitra mampisaraka anareo amin’Andriamanitrareo.” (Isaia 59:2a)
"The wages of sin is death" [spiritual separation from God]. (Romans 6:23) “Fa fahafatesana [fisarahana ara-panahy amin’Andriamanitra] no tambin’ny ota.” (Romana 6:23)
This picture illustrates that God is holy and people are sinful. A great gap separates the two. The arrows illustrate that people are continually trying to reach God and the abundant life through their own efforts, such as a good life, philosophy, or religion – but they always fail. Io sary io dia mampiseho fa masina Andriamanitra ary mpanota ny olombelona. Hantsana lehibe no mampisaraka azy amin’Andriamanitra. Miezaka araka izay saim-pantany izy mba hanana fiainana tonga lafatra, mba ho tsara fitondran-tena, ho tsara fanahy sy hanampy ny hafa sns… Tsy mahafaka ny fahotany anefa izany.
The third law explains the only way to bridge this gap… Ny zava-dehibe fahatelo no manome antsika ny vahaolana amin’izany…
3

LAW THREE

Jesus Christ is God’s ONLY provision for our sin. Through Him alone you can know and experience God’s love and plan for your life.

 3

FAHATELO

I Jeso Kristy irery ihany no manafaka amin’ny fahotana . Maty noho ny fahotantsika izy ary amin’ny alalany no ahazoantsika mahafantatra sy mivelona amin’ny fitiavan’ Andriamanitra sy ny fiainana atolony.
He Died in Our Place to save us… MATY HAMONJY ANTSIKA IZY...
"God demonstrates His own love toward us, in that while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us." (Romans 5:8) “Fa Andriamanitra mampiseho ny fitiavany antsika, fa fony mbola mpanota isika, dia maty hamonjy antsika Kristy”. (Romana 5:8)
… and Rose From the Dead ...ARY NITSANGANA TAMIN’NY MATY.
"Christ died for our sins…He was buried…He was raised on the third day, according to the Scriptures…He appeared to Peter, then to the Twelve. After that He appeared to more than five hundred…" (1 Corinthians 15:3-6) “Kristy maty noho ny fahotantsika ... dia nalevina, ary natsangana tamin’ny andro fahatelo araka ny Soratra Masina… ary niseho tamin’i Kefasy Izy, dia vao tamin’ny roa ambin’ny folo lahy koa. Rehefa afaka izany, dia niseho tamin’ny rahalahy tsy omby dimanjato Izy…” (1 Korintiana 15:3-6)
He Is the Only Way to God IZY NO LALANA TOKANA.
"Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father but through Me.'" (John 14:6) Hoy i Jeso: “Izaho no làlana sy fahamarinana ary fiainana, tsy misy olona mankany amin’ny Ray, afa-tsy amin’nyalalako.”(Jaona 14:6)
This picture illustrates that it is only God who is able to re-establish our relationship with Him, and this has been achieved by sending His Son, Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place to pay the penalty for our sins. Io sary io dia mampiseho fa Andriamanitra irery no mamerina amintsika ny fifandraisana Aminy. Izany dia nataony tamin’ny alalan’i Jeso Kristy izay maty nisolo voina antsika teo amin’ny hazo fijaliana.
It is not enough just to know these three laws... Tsy ampy anefa ny mahalala ireo zava-dehibe telo voalaza ireo fa …

Do Not rely on your FeelingsAZA MITOKY AMIN’NY FAHATSAPANAO
The promise of God's Word, the Bible - not our feelings - is our authority. The Christian lives by faith (trust) in the trustworthiness of God Himself and His Word. This train diagram illustrates the relationship among fact (God and His Word), faith (our trust in God and His Word), and feeling (the result of our faith and obedience). (Read John 14:21)Ny Tenin’Andriamanitra no itokiantsika fa tsy ny fahatsapana. Ny Kristiana dia mivelona amin’ny finoana (fahatokiana) an’ Andriamanitra sy ny Teniny. Ny sary eto ambany dia manazava ny fifandraisana eo amin’ny fahamarinana (Andriamanitra sy ny Teniny), sy ny finoana (fahatokiana an’Andriamanitra sy ny Teniny) ary ny (ny vokatry ny finoana sy ny fankatoavana). (Vakio ny Jaona 14:21).
The train will run with or without a passenger car. However, it would be useless to attempt to pull the train by the passenger car. In the same way, as Christians we do not depend on feelings or emotions to decide what is true, but we place our faith (trust) in the trustworthiness of God and the promises of His Word.Afaka mandeha ny lamasinina na misy na tsy misy kalesy. Kanefa, ny kalesy kosa dia tsy afaka hitarika ny lamasinina. Dia toy izany koa, isika Kristiana dia tsy miantehatra amin’ny fihetseham-po na ny fahatsapana fa mametraka ny finoantsika (fahatokiana) amin’Andriamanitra sy ny Teniny.

Suggestions for Christian GrowthNY FITOMBOANA ARA-PANAHY
Spiritual growth results from trusting Jesus Christ. "...The righteous man shall live by faith" (Galatians 3:11). A life of faith will enable you to trust God increasingly with every detail of your life, and to practice the following: Ny fitomboana ara-panahy dia vokatry ny fahatokiana an’i Jeso Kristy. “Ny marina amin’ny finoana no ho velona “ (Galatiana 3:11). Ny fiainam-pinoana dia hahatonga anao hitombo amin’ny fahatokiana an’Andriamanitra eo amin’ny lafim-piainanao rehetra ary hanatanteraka an’ireto:
  • Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7)
  • Manokàna fotoana isan’andro hivavahanao amin’Andriamanitra (Jaona 15:7).
  • Read God's Word daily. (see Acts 17:11) Begin with the Gospel of John.
  • Mandiniha ny Tenin’Andriamanitra isan’andro (Asa 17:11). Atombohy amin’ny Filazantsaran’i Jaona izany.
  • Obey God moment by moment. (see John 14:21)
  • Ankatoavy mandrakariva ny Tenin’Andriamanitra (Jaona 14:21).
  • Witness for Christ by your life and words. (see Matthew 4:19 and John 15:8)
  • Mitoria an’i Kristy amin’ny toetranao sy ny teny ataonao (Mat 4:19; Jaona 15:8).
  • Trust God for every detail of your life. (1 Peter 5:7)
  • Matokia an’Andriamanitra eo amin’ny lafim-piainanao rehetra (I Pet 5:7).
  • Holy Spirit – allow Him to control and empower your daily life and witness (Galatians 5:16,17; Acts 1:8).
  • Aoka hotarihin ’ny Fanahy Masina ianao eo amin’ny fiainanao andavanandro sy eo amin’ny fijoroanao ho vavolombelona (Galatiana 5:16,17; Asa 1:8).
Fellowship in a Good ChurchNY MAHA-ZAVA-DEHIBE NY FIANGONANA
The Bible tells us the importance of meeting together with other Christians (Hebrews 10:25).
Have you ever watched a fire burning in a fireplace? Several logs together burn brightly. But if you pull one log away from the fire, its flame soon goes out. The same thing happens to us if we do not spend time with other Christians. Attend a church where Christ is talked about and the Bible is taught. Start this week and make plans to attend regularly.		Ny Tenin’Andriamanitra dia mananatra antsika mba tsy hahafoy ny fiarahantsika miangona (Hebreo 10:25). Raha maromaro ny kitay ao am-patana dia mirehitra tsara ny afo. Raha atokana toerana anefa ny kitay anankiray dia mihamaty ny afo aminy. Dia toy izany koa, ho sarotra aminao ny hivelona samy irery ny fiainana kristiana. Zava-dehibe tokoa ny hifaneraseranao amin’olona hafa izay efa nandray an’i Jeso Kristy ho Tompony sy Mpamonjy azy. Noho izany, aoka ianao hanatona fiaraha-mivavaka any am-piangonana, dia izay fiangonana anekena fa Tenin’Andriamanitra marina ny Baiboly manontolo ary ankalazana an’i Jeso Kristy. Atombohy amin’ity herinandro ity izany dia manaova planina mba hanarahanao hatrany izany fiaraha-mivavaka izany.
