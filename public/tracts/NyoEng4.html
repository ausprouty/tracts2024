Have You Heard of the Four Spiritual Laws? OMAZIRE KUHURRA? EBINTU EBIKURU EBY'OMWOYO.
1

Law One

God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

1

EKIRAGIRO EKYOKUBANZA

Ruhanga nakugonza kandi anyina entegeka yamahano habwobwomeezi bwawe.

GOD’S LOVE OKUGONZA KWA RUHANGA
“God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish, but have eternal life” (John 3:16 NIV). Baitu Ruhanga nukwo yagondeze ati Ensi, nahayo Omwanawe ayazairwe omu wenka, Omuntu weena anyakumwikiriza aleke okubura baitu atunge obwomeezi obutahwaho. (Yohana 3:16)
GOD’S PLAN EKIGENDERERWA KYA RUHANGA
[Christ speaking] “I came that they might have life, and might have it abundantly” (John 10:10). Yesu Kristo nagamba, "Nyowe nkaija zibe n'obwomeezi kandi zibe nabwo obwingi." (Yohana 10:10).
Why aren’t all people experiencing the abundant life? Tibali abantu boona abamanyire ngu banyina obwomeezi bunu obwingi.
2

Law Two

Man is SINFUL and SEPARATED from God. Thus, he cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for his life.]

2

EKIRAGIRO EKYAKABIRI

Omuntu musiisi kandi ekibikye kikamwahukaniza na Ruhanga; nahabweki tasobora kumanya okugonza n'entegeka ya Ruhanga habw'obwomeezibwe.

MAN IS SINFUL OMUNTU MUSIISI
“All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God” (Romans 3:23). Baitu boona bakasiisa kandi tibahikireire ha kitinisa kya Ruhanga. (Abarumi 3:23)
Man was created to be friends with God; but he chose to go his own way, and friendship with God was broken. This is what the Bible calls sin. This is shown: Omuntu akahangwa kukorrana na Ruhanga baitu habwobweremebwe akacwamu kukwata omuhanda gwe wenka, kandi kinu Baibuli nikyo eyeta ekibi. Okuruga obu, obumu bw'omuntu na Ruhanga bukasisikara. Kinu nikyeyoleka
(1) when people rebel against God, (i) omuntu obwayeremera Ruhanga,
(2) when people do not care about God and (ii) omuntu obwatafayo hali Ruhanga,
(3) when people do not live up to God's perfect standar. (iii) omuntu obwatahika harulengo oruhikire orwa Ruhanga.
MAN IS SEPARATED OMUNTU AKAHUKANA NA RUHANGA
“But your iniquities have separated you from God.” (Isaiah 59:2) Ebyokutahikirra byanyu nibyo bibaganiize inywe na Ruhanga wanyu. (Isaya 59:2)
God is holy and man is sinful. There is a great separation between the two. The arrows show how man tries to reach God and the abundant life through his own efforts: such as living a good life, being religious, helping others etc., but all his efforts fail. Ruhanga ali Arukwera baitu omuntu misiisi. Omuntu alengaho kuhika hali Ruhanga omu miringo etali emu, gamba nkokulinda ebiragiro, kuba wengeso nungi, nokuserra ebyamagezi n'ebindi, baitu tibimugasa habwokuba haroho ekiinongoro kikooto ekyekibi hagatiye na Ruhanga.
The third law gives the only answer to this problem… Ekiragiro ekyakasatu nikitwoleka nkoku haroho omuhanda gumu gwonka ogwokukirramu:
3

Law Three

Jesus Christ is God’s ONLY provision for man’s sin. Through Him you can know and experience God’s love and plan for your life.

3

EKIRAGIRO EKYAKASATU

Ruhanga akahayo Yesu Kristo okuba omuhanda omuntu ogwarabamu okugaruka hali Ruhanga. Yesu Kristo akafa habwaitu. Omu Yesu honka omuntu nuho amanyira okugonza kwa Ruhanga nentegekaye habwobwomeezi bwawe.

HE DIED IN OUR PLACE AKAFA OMU KIIKARO KYAITU
“For Christ also suffered once for sins, the righteous for the unrighteous, to bring you to God. He was put to death in the body but made alive in the Spirit." (1 Peter 3:18) Baitu na Kristo akabonabonesibwa obw'ebibi omurundi gumu, ahikriire habwa abatali abahikiriire atuleta hali Ruhanga. (1 Petero 3:18)
HE ROSE FROM THE DEAD AKAHUMBUKA NAHATI MWOMEEZI
“After his suffering, he presented himself to them and gave many convincing proofs that he was alive. He appeared to them over a period of forty days and spoke about the kingdom of God.” (Acts 1:3). Kandi obuyamazire kusalizibwa yayeyoleka ayomiire halibo nobwokurorraho bwingi kandi yarorwaga ebiro amakumi ana, nagamba ebyobukama bwa Ruhanga. (Ebikorwa by Abakwenda 1:3)
HE IS THE ONLY WAY NUWE WENKA MUHANDA
“Jesus said to him, ‘I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father, but through Me.’” (John 14:6) Yesu yamugambira ati, "Ninyowe muhanda n'amananu n'obwomeezi, busaho aija hali Isenyowe rundi arabire omuli nyowe." (Yohana 14:6)
God has bridged this separation between us and Himself by sending His Son Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place. Okufa kwa Yesu Kristo ha musalaba kukatinda ekiinongoro ekyekibi ekitwahukaniza na Ruhanga. Hati tunyina obugabe kwambuka n'okuhika owa Ruhanga.
It is not enough just to know these three laws … Kumanya bumanya ebintu binu bisatu tikirukumara.
4

Law Four

We must individually RECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

4

EKIRAGIRO EKYAKANA

Titusobora kumanya ekigendererwa kya Ruhanga habw'obwomeezi bwaitu rundi buli, buli omu aikirize Yesu Kristo kuba Mujuniwe kandi akamworobera nka Mukamawe.

WE MUST RECEIVE CHRIST NITUSEMERRA OKWIKIRIZA YESU KRISTO
“As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, even to those who believe in His name” (John 1:12) "Baitu boona abamwikiriize akabaha obusobozi okufooka abaana ba Ruhanga."(Yohaana 1:12)
WE MUST RECEIVE CHRIST YESU KRISTO OMU KWIKIRIZA
“By grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God; not as a result of works, that no one should boast” (Ephesians 2:8,9). Baitu mukajunwa habwembabazi obwokwikiriza,i kunu tibuli obwanyu inywenka; nikyo kisembo kya Ruhanga ekitaruga omu mirimo, omuntu weena aleke okwenyumiza. (Abefeso 2:8,9)
WE RECEIVE CHRIST THROUGH PERSONAL INVITATION TWIKIRIZA YESU KRISTO OBUTUMUKINGURRA OKUTAHA OMU BWOMEZI BWAITU, BULl OMU
[Christ speaking] “Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if any one hears My voice and opens the door, I will come in to him.” (Revelation 3:20) Kristo nagamba, "Dora nyemeriire ha rwigi ninyeranga: Omuntu weena obwalihurra iraka lyange nakinguraho orwigi nditaha namberali." (Okusukururw 3:20)

Oratahize Yesu Kristo omu bwomeezi bwawe?
‎(Which heart best represents your life?)‎

Wakugondeze kutaahya Yesu Kristo omu bwomeezi bwawe?
‎(Which heart would you like to have represent your life?)‎

Ebirukwongeraho nibisoborra nkoku orukusobora kutaahya Yesu Kristo omu bwomeezi bwawe:
‎(The following explains how you can receive Christ:)‎

YOU CAN RECEIVE CHRIST RIGHT NOW BY FAITH THROUGH PRAYER NOSOBORA OKWIKIRIZA YESU KRISTO OKUTAAHA OMU BWOMEEZI BWAWE HATI BUNU, OMU KUSABA
(Prayer is talking to God) (omu kusaba nuho tubaliza na Ruhanga)
God knows your heart and is not so concerned with your words as He is with the attitude of your heart. The following is a suggested prayer: Ruhanga tarukufaayo ebigambo ebyorukukozesa baitu amananu gomumutuma gwawe. Nosobora kusaba oti:
“Lord Jesus, I need You. I have been controlling my own life and have sinned against You. Thank You for forgiving my sins. Come into my life and take control of my life. Make me the kind of person You want me to be.” Mukama wange Yesu Kristo ninkwetaaga. Nkingwire orwigi rwobwomeezi bwange otaheemu onjune. Webale kunganyira ebibi byange. Kuruga hati olemege obwomeezi bwange kandi onfole nkoku orukugonza. Amina.
Does this prayer express the desire of your heart? Ekyokusaba kinu nikyoleka okwetaaga kwomutuma gwawe?
If it does, I invite you to pray this prayer right now, and Christ will come into your life, as He promised. Obukiraaba nukwo kiri, saba oti nukwo Yesu Kristo ataahe omu bwomeezi bwawe.
THE BIBLE PROMISES ETERNAL LIFE TO ALL WHO RECEIVE CHRIST BOONA ABAIKIRIZA YESU KRISTO KUTAAHA OMU BWOMEEZI BWABU RUHANGA ABAHA OBWOMEEZI OBUTAHWAHO
"And this is the testimony: God has given us eternal life, and this life is in his Son. Whoever has the Son has life; whoever does not have the Son of God does not have life." (1 John 5:11-12) Kandi kunu nukwo kumanyisa ngu Ruhanga akatuha obwomeezi otutahwaho, n'obwomeezi bunu buli omu Mwanawe. Ogu anyakwina Omwana nuwe anyina obwomeezi obu: atanyina Omwana wa Ruhanga tanyina obwomeezi obu. (1 Yohana 5:11-12)
Did you receive Christ into your life? NOMANYIRA NKAHA NGU YESU KRISTO ATAAHIRE OMU BWOMEEZI BWAWE?
Christ said that He would come into your life. Would He mislead you? Yesu Kristo nanihiza kutaaha omu bwomeezi bwogu weena anyakukinguraho orwigi. Iwe okingwirehoga orwigi rwobwomezi bwawe? Hati ali nkaha? Nogumiza nkaha ngu Ruhanga agarukiremu okusaba kwawe? Ruhanga wamananu kandi ekigambokye kyokwesiga.
Thank God often that Christ is in your life and that He will never leave you (Hebrews 13:5). You can know that Christ lives in you and that you have eternal life because of His promise. Nuwe wenka yagambire ati. "Tindikuleka nakake kunu tindikuhemukira nakake." (Abaheburaniya 13:5)
DO NOT DEPEND ON FEELINGS OKWEHULIRIZA OMU MUTUMA
The promise of God’s Word, the Bible - not our feelings - is our authority. The Christian lives by faith (trust) in the trustworthiness of God Himself and His Word. Obwomeezi bw'Omukristo bubaho habwokwikiriza Ruhanga ebya baza naitwe omu kitabu Ekirukwera. Emirago ya Ruhanga nubwo obusobozi bwaitu. Ekisisani ky'egaali y'omwika kinu nikiragirra Baibuli, okwikiriza n'okwehuliriza nkoku bikorwa ahamu.
The train will run with or without a guard's van. However, it would be useless to attempt to pull the train by the guard's van. In the same way, as Christians we do not depend on feelings or emotions, but we place our faith (trust) in the trustworthiness of God and the promises of His Word. Egaali y'omwika enu nesobora okugenda nekiju kyomulinzi rundi obundi kitaroho. Tikisoboka egali enu okusikwa nokozesa ekiju kyomulinzi. Omu mulingo nugwo gumu abakristo tibesiga ikwehuliriza kwabu. Ntusemerra okuta okwikiriza kwaitu omu ebi Ruhanga ebyagamba omu Baibuli.
NOW THAT YOU HAVE RECEIVED CHRIST HATI OMZIRE OKWIKIRIZA KRISTO OMU BWOMEEZI BWAWE
The moment that you received Christ by faith, as an act of the will, many things happened, including the following: Omu kasumi oikiririzaho Kristo, ebintu bingi bibaireho:
1. Christ came into your life (Revelation 3:20, Colossians 1:27) 1. Kristo amazire okutaaha omu bwomeezi bwawe. (Okusukururwa 3:20, Abakolosai 1:27)
2. Your sins were forgiven (Colossians 1:14). 2. Ebibi byawe bimazire okuganyirwa. (Abakolosai 1:14)
3. You became a child of God (John 1:12). 3. Hati ofokere Omwana we Ruhanga. (Yohana 1:12)
4. You began the great adventure for which God created you (John 10:10, 2 Corinthians 5:14, 15, 17). 4. Hati noija kutandika okumanya entegeka ya Ruhanga nekigendererwa kye habw'obwomeezi bwawe. (Yohana 10:10; 2 Abakolinso 5:14,15,17)
Can you think of anything more wonderful that could happen to you than receiving Christ? Would you like to thank God in prayer right now for what He has done for you? By thanking God, you demonstrate your faith. Nosobora okutekerezayo ekintu kyona ekikuru ekisobora kukuhikaho okukira okutunga Yesu? Wakugondeze okusiima Ruhanga hati omukusaba habweki ekyakukoliire? Okusiima Ruhanga nikiragirra okwikiriza kwawe.
SUGGESTIONS FOR CHRISTIAN GROWTH HATI HASIGALIREKI?
Spiritual growth results from trusting Jesus Christ. EBYOKWETAAGA KUKURA OMU MWOYO
Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7). Baza na Ruhanga buli kiro omu kusab. (Yohana 15:7)
Read God’s Word daily (Acts 17:11); begin with the Gospel of John. Leka Ruhanga abaze naiwe buli kiro omu kusoma ekigambokye, kandi kihondere. (Ebikorwa by'Abakwenda 17:11)
Meet regularly with other believers for worship and encouragment (Hebrews 10:25). Terana n'abandi abataahize Yesu Kristo omu bwomeezi bwabu. (Abaheburaniya 10:25)
Have God control and empower your daily life and witness (Acts 1:8). Manyisa abandi ebya Yesu Kristo, omu bikorwa byawe n'omu bigambo byawe. (Ebikorwa by'Abakwenda 1:8)
Trust God for every detail of your life (1 Peter 5:7). Yesiga Ruhanga omu byona. (1 Petero 5:7)
Obey God moment by moment (John 14:21). Ikiriza okwerobera Ruhanga buli kasumi. (Yohana 14:21)
