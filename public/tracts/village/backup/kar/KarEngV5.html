A PRAYER OF FAITH TUG NTSEEG ZAAJ LUG THOV
(Prayer is talking with God.) (Kev thov Vaajtswv yog nrug Vaajtswv sis thaam)
God knows what you want to say and if you truly mean it. Vaajtswv yeej paub tej kws koj xaav has hab saib koj puas has tseeb tag.
THIS IS THE PRAYER YOU CAN USE. KOJ SIV ZAAJ LUG THOV NUAV LOS TAU.
‘Jesus, I need you in my life. I believe in you as my Saviour and Lord. Thank you for forgiving my sins and giving me eternal life. Take control of my life. Make me the kind of person you want me to be. Amen’ ‘Tswv Yexu, kuv xaav tau koj lug nyob huv kuv lub neej. Kuv ntseeg Koj ua kuv tug Cawmseej hab tug Tswv. Ua tsaug Koj tau zaam kuv tej kev txhum hab muab txujsa ntev rua kuv. Thov Koj tswj kuv lub neej. Thov Koj hloov kuv ua tug tuabneeg kws koj xaav kuas kuv ua. Asmee.
Is this prayer what you would like to say to God? Zaaj lug thov nuav puas yog zaaj koj xaav has rua Vaajtswv?
If it is, would you like to pray this prayer right now? Yog hastas yog, koj puas kaam has zaaj lug thov nuav taamsim nuav?
WHAT JESUS HAS DONE FOR YOU TEJ YEXU TAU UA RUA KOJ
When you believe in Jesus as your Saviour and Lord you can be sure that: Thaum koj ntseeg Yexu ua koj tug Cawmseej hab tug Tswv, koj yeej paub tseeb hastas:
  1. Jesus came into your life. (Revelations 3:30, Colossians 1:27)
  2. Your sins were forgiven. (Colossians 1:14)
  3. You became a child of God. (John 1:12)
  4. You have eternal life. (John 5:24)
  5. You began the new life for which God made you. (John 10:10)
  1. Kheto lug rua huv koj lub neej (Tshwmsim 3:30; Khaulauxai 1:27)
  2. Vaajtswv tau zaam koj tej kev txhum lawm (Khaulauxai 1:14)
  3. Koj yog Vaajtswv ib tug mivnyuam (Yauhaa 1:12)
  4. Koj tau txujsa ntev (Yauhaa 5:24)
  5. Koj cuab ua lub neej tshab kws Vaajtswv coj koj moog ua (Yauhaa 10:10)
NOW YOU SHOULD: ZAG NUAV KOJ YUAV TAU:
Go to a church where Jesus Christ is truly worshipped and where God’s Word (the Bible) is taught. Moog koom ib pawg-ntseeg kws hawm Yexu Kheto hab qha Vaajtswv Txujlug tseeb.
Read God’s Word daily or find someone who will read it to you. Koj nyeem Vaajtswv Txujlug txhua nub losyog nrhav ib tug tuabneeg nyeem rua koj.
