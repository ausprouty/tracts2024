Txhua tus yog tibneeg txhaum. Peb ua tej yam uas tsis yog thiab tej no ua rau peb txav deb ntawm Vajtswv. Peb paub tsistau thiab zoo siab tsis tau rau Vajtswv txojkev hlub thiab lub tswvyim rau peb lub neej.

PEOPLE ARE SINFUL TIBNEEG MUAJ KEV TXHAUM
‘All people have sinned.’ (They are not living the way God wants them to live.) Romans 3:23 “Txhua tus tibneeg tau ua txhaum.’ (Lawv tsis ua neej li uas Vajtswv hais kom lawv ua). Loos 3:23
People were created to be friends of God, but because we wanted to please ourselves, we chose to go our own way without God. Our friendship with God was spoiled. This going our own way means doing things that God does not want us to do or taking no interest in God. This is what the Bible calls sin. Vajtswv tsim tibneeg nrog Vajtswv ua phoojywg, tiamsis vim peb tsuas xav ua kom zoo peb lub siab xwb, peb tau xaiv taug peb txojkev tsis muaj Vajtswv. Peb txojkev nrog Vajtswv ua phoojywg tau puastsuaj lawm. Qhov peb taug peb txojkev no yog peb ua tej uas Vajtswv tsis xav kom peb ua losyog tsis quavntsej txog Vajtswv. Vajlugkub hu qhov no haistias yog kev txhaum.
PEOPLE ARE KEPT A LONG WAY FROM GOD. TIBNEEG RAUG CAIS DEB NTAWM VAJTSWV.
"The payment for sin is death." Romans 6:23 “Tus nqi them kev txhaum yog kev tuag.” Loos 6:23
This death is more than just our body dying – it means spiritual death – being kept a long way from God and not having any friendship with Him. Txojkev tuag no yog tshaj li uas peb lub cev tuag xwb – nws yog tuag sab ntsujplig thiab – uas yog raug cais deb ntawm Vajtswv thiab ua tsis tau phoojywg nrog Vajtswv.
God is holy and good while people are sinful. The difference between God and people is so great it is like a great, deep valley keeping them a long way from each other. People are always trying to reach God and find the full, best life by doing things themselves, such as trying to be good, obeying the laws of the land and being kind to others. But people can never reach God by their own effort. Vajtswv yog tus dawbhuv thiab zoo tiamsis tibneeg yog cov muaj kev txhaum. Qhov txawv ntawm Vajtswv thiab tibneeg loj heev ib yam li ib lub kwjha loj thiab tob uas cais ob leeg lawm. Tiabneeg tabmeeg xav mus cuag Vajtswv kom tau lub neej npuvnpo, lub neej zoo lawv tus kheej ua, xws li ua tibneeg zoo, coj raws li kevcai ntiajteb thiab ua zoo rau lwm tus. Tiamsis tibneeg yeej mus cuag tsis tau Vajtswv dhau los ntawm lawv tus dag zog.
