Have you heard of the Four Spiritual Laws? Mbese hari ubwo wumvise... Amagambo ane y’ingenzi Imana ishaka ko umenya?
Just as there are physical laws that govern the physical universe, so are there spiritual laws that govern your relationship with God. Nk’uko buri kintu cyose gifite ubumwe buyobora imibereho yacyo, ni ko n’imibereho y’umwuka ifite ubumwe bugomba gukurikizwa kugira ngo ugirane ubumwe n’lmana.
1

God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

1

Imana IRAGUKUNDA, kandi ifitiye imibereho yawe IMIGAMBI itangaje

God's Love URUKUNDO RW’IMANA
“God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish, but have eternal life” (John 3:16 NIV). “Kuko Imana yakunze abari mw’isi cyane, byatumye itang’Umwana wayo w’ikinege kugira ng’umwizera wes’ atarimbuka, ahubwo ahabwe ubugingo bohoraho” (Yohana 3:16).
God's Plan IMIGAMBI IMANA IGUFITIYE
Jesus said, "I came that they may have life, and have it abundantly" [that it might be full and meaningful]." (John 10:10) “Ariko njyeweho, nazanywe no kugira ngo zibone ubugingo, ndetse ngo zibone bwinshi” (Yohana 10:10).
Why is it that most people are not experiencing the abundant life? Because... Mbese ni kuki hari abantu benshi badafite ubwo bugingo?
Because… Ni uko...
2

People are SINFUL and SEPARATED from God so we cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for our life.

2

Muntu nl UMUNYABYAHA, kandi ibyaha bye BYAMUTANDUKANIJE n’Imana bituma atamenya urukundo n’imigambi Imana ifitiye imibereho ye.

People are Sinful UMUNTU N’UMUNYABYAHA
"All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23) “Kuko bose bakoze ibyaha ntibashyikira ubwiza bw’lmana” (Abaroma 3:23)
We were created to have a personal relationship with God, but by our own choice and self-will we have gone our own independent way and that relationship has been broken. This self-will, often seen as an attitude of active rebellion towards God or a lack of interest in Him, is an evidence of what the Bible calls sin. Umuntu yaremewe kugirana ubumwe n’lmana. Ariko umuntu yanyuranije n’imigambi y’lmana ahitamo kuyigomera, ntiyayumvira. Kugomera Imana ni ko gukora icyaha. Iyo umuntu anyuranyije n’iby’lmana ishaka akomeza kuba mu mibereho y’ibyaha.
People are Separated UMUNTU YATANDUKANYE N’IMANA
"But your iniquities have separated you from your God…" (Isaiah 59:2) “Ahubwo gukiranirwa kwanyu ni ko kwabatandukanije n’lmana yanyu” (yesaya 59:2)
This picture illustrates that God is holy and people are sinful. A great gap separates the two. The arrows illustrate that people are continually trying to reach God and the abundant life through their own efforts, such as a good life, philosophy, or religion – but they always fail. Imana irera. Umuntu ni umunyabyaha. Hagati ye n’lmana hari Umworera w’ibyaha wamutandukanije n’lmana. Umuntu agerageza gukoresha uburyo bwinshi bwamugeza ku Mana, agashakira inzira mw’idini, mu masengesho, mu mirimo myiza n’ibindi. Ariko ibyo byose ntibihagije, umuntu aba akiri umunyabyaha.
The third law explains the only way to bridge this gap… ariho inzira imwe ihuza umuntu n’lmana
3

Jesus Christ is God’s ONLY provision for our sin. Through Him alone you can know and experience God’s love and plan for your life.

3

Yesu Kristo nl we nzira Y’UKURI igez’umuntu ku Mana, yaradupfiriye, muriwe nl ho ushobora kumenyera urukundo n’imigambi Imana ifitiye imibereho yawe.

HE DIED IN OUR PLACE YARAPFUYE KANDI ARAZUKA, KUGIRA NGO ATUGEZE KU MANA
"For Christ died for sins once for all,the righteous for the unrighteous, to bring you to God." (1 Peter 3:18) “Kuko na Kristo yababarijwe ibyaha by’abantu rimwe, umukiranutsi ababarizwa abakiranirwa, kugira ngo atuyobore ku Mana” (1 Petero 3:18)
HE ROSE FROM THE DEAD YESU NL MUZIMA
"Christ died for our sins…He was buried…He was raised on the third day, according to the Scriptures…He appeared to Peter, then to the Twelve. After that He appeared to more than five hundred…" (1 Corinthians 15:3-6) "Muzi ko nabanje kubaha ibyo nanjye nahawe kumenya, yuko Kristo yapfiriye ibyaha byacu nk’uko byari byaranditswe, agahambwa, akazuka ku munsi wa gatatu, nk’uko byari byaranditswe” (1 Abakorinto 15:3,4)
He Is the Only Way to God YESU NIWE NZIRA WENYINE
"Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father but through Me.'" (John 14:6) “Yesu aramubgira ati: Ni jye nzira, n’ukuri, n’ubugingo: Nta ujya kwa Data ntamujyanye” (Yohana 14:6)
This diagram illustrates that God has bridged the gulf that separates us from Him by sending His Son, Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place to pay the penalty for our sins. Umuntu ntabasha kwigeza ku Mana, byatumye itanga umwana wayo, arapfa, abambwa ku musaraba kugira ngo atubere urutindo rutugeza ku Mana
It is not enough just to know these three laws... Ijambo rya kane riragusobanurira uko ushobora kumenya Imana mu mibereho yawe…

We must individually RECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

WE MUST RECEIVE CHRIST UKWIRIYE KWAKIRA UMWAMI YESU MU BURYO BWO KUMWIZERA
"As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, even to those who believe in His name." (John 1:12) “Icyakora abamwemeye bose, bakizera izina rye,yabahaye ubushobezi bwo kaba abana b’lmana” (Yohana 1:12)
"By grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God; not as a result of works so that no one may boast." (Ephesians 2:8,9) “Mwakijijwe n’ubuntu kubwo kwizera: Ntibyavuye kuri mwe, ahubwo ni impano y’lmana. Ntibyavuye no ku mirimo kugira ngo hatagira umuntu wirarira” (Abefeso 2:8,9)
WE RECEIVE CHRIST THROUGH FAITH WE RECEIVE CHRIST BY PERSONAL INVITATION KWAKIRA YESU, N’UKUMUKINGURIRA AKINJIRA MU MUTIMA WAWE
[Christ speaking] "Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if anyone hears My voice and opens the door, I will come in to him." (Revelation 3:20) “Dore mpagaze k’urugi, ndakomanga. Umuntu niyumv’ijwi ryanjye, agakingur’urugi, nzinjir’iwe, dusangire” (Ibyahishuwe 3:20)
To accept Jesus Christ is: Kwakir’Umwami Yesu:
To acknowledge that you are a sinner, repent, and agree that Jesus died on the cross in your place. N’ukumenya ko urtumunyabyaha, ukihana, ugahindukilir’lmana
To believe that God has forgiven you all your sins. N’ukwilingir’lmana ko ishobora kukubabalira rwose.
To allow Jesus Christ to be in charge of your whole life so that you can live the way God wants you to live. N’ukwakira Yesu Kristo mu bugingo bwawe umwizeye, akagutegeka kugira ngo ujy’ukora iby’ashaka.

You Can Receive Christ Right Now by Faith Through Prayer USHOBORA KWAKIRA YESU NONAHA
God knows your heart and is not so concerned with your words as He is with the attitude of your heart. The following is a suggested prayer: SabaYesu yinjire mu bugingo bwawe.Umwilingire ko ashobora kugukorera icyo yasezeranye. Dore isengesho lishobora kugufasha kwakira Yesu, n’urivuga n’umutima ukunze. Senga uti:
Lord Jesus, I need You. Thank you for dying on the cross for my sins. I open the door of my life and receive You as my Savior and Lord. Thank You for forgiving my sins and giving me eternal life. Take control of the throne of my life. Make me the kind of person You want me to be. Mwami Yesu ngwino mu bugingo bwaanje. Ibyaha byanjye byantandukanje nawe ndakinginze untegeke. Ngushimiye ko umbabliye kandi kuv’ubunemeye ko ndi umunyabyaha. Ndashaka kubana nawe. Hindura imibereho yanjye yose mbe nk’uko ushaka. Amen.
Does this prayer express the desire of your heart? If it does, I invite you to pray this prayer right now. Mbese, har’ubwo wumv’ili sengesho lihuje n’icy’umutima wawe ushaka?
Christ will come into your life as He promised. Ngaho niba ili sengesho lihuje n’icy’umutima wawe ushaka, ngaho noneho livuge usabe Umwami Yesu kwinjira mu bugingo bwawe.
HOW TO KNOW THAT CHRIST IS IN YOUR LIFE UBURYO USHOBORA KUMENYA KO YESU ALI MU BUGINGO BWAWE
Did you receive Christ into your life? According to His promise in Revelation 3:20, where is Christ right now in relation to you? Christ said that He would come into your life. Would He mislead you? Mbese, wakiriye Yesu mu bugingo bwawe? Ubiz’ute? Ubu ali hehe? Ibuka uko mu Byahishuwe 3:20 havuga. Yesu yevuze ko azinjira i wawe. Si koko se?
On what authority do you know that God has answered your prayer? (The trustworthiness of God Himself and His Word.) None se umenye ute ko Imana yumvise gusenga kwawe? Imana yadusezeranije ko icyo tuzayisaba cyose tuyizeye izakiduha.
The Bible promises Eternal Life to all who receive Christ. UFITE YESU ABAFITE UBUGINGO BUHORAHO
"God has given us eternal life, and this life is in His Son. He who has the Son has the life; he who does not have the Son of God does not have the life. These things I have written to you who believe in the name of the Son of God, so that you may know that you have eternal life." (1 John 5:11-13) “Kandiuko guhamya ni uku, ni uko Imana yaduhaye ubugingo bahoraho, kandi ubwo bugingo bubonerwa mu Mwana wayo. Ufite uwo Mwana ni we ufite ubwo bugingo; naho udafite Umwana w’lmana nta bugingo afite. Ibyo ndabibandikiye, mwebwe abizeye izina ry’Umwana w’lmana, kugira ngo mumenye ko mufite ubugingo buhoraho.” (1 Yohana 5: 11-13
Thank God often that Christ is in your life and that He will never leave you (Hebrews 13:5). You can know on the basis of His promise that Christ lives in you and that you have eternal life from the very moment you invite Him in. He will not deceive you. Ujye ushima Imana buri munsi. Uyishimire ko Yesu ari mu bungingo bwawe. Umenye neza rwose ko atazagusiga wenyine (Matayo 28:20; Abaheburayo 13:5). Ushobora kumenya ko Yesu abana nawe kandi ko ufite ubugingo buhoraho nk’uko yabisezeranye.
Do Not rely on your Feelings NTIWISHINGIKIRIZE KU BY’UMUTIMA WAWE WIBWIRA
It is possible to feel joyful one day and depressed the next. No matter how you feel Jesus does not change in your life. (Hebrews 13:5) Ushobora kugira umunezero ubundi ukagira umubabaro. Uko uzaba uri kose, umenye ko Yesu adahinduka. (Abaheburayo 13:5)
  • The truth of God has power to guide you.
  • Ukuri kw’lmana ni ko mbaraga zituyobora.
  • We are joined by the power of truth through faith
  • Kwizera ni ko kuduhesha imbaraga z’ukuri.
  • When we obey the truth our hearts are guided correctly.
  • Iyo twomviye ukuri, ntidutegekwa n’ibyo twibwira.
  • We depend on God and His truth, not how we feel.
  • Twilingira Imana n’ukuri kwayo kuruta ibyo twibwira.
If we sin we repent and he forgives us. Iyo dukoze icyaha, turacyihana, Imana nayo ikatubabarira. Nuko rero, ntukishingikirize kuby’utekereza, ahubwo wishingikirize ukuri kw’Ijambo ry’lmana kubwo kwizera.
NOW THAT YOU HAVE RECEIVED CHRIST UBWO UMAZE KWAKIRA YESU
The moment you received Christ by faith, as an act of the will, many things happened, including the following: Hari ibintu bishya byabonetse mu bugingo bwawe:
  • Christ came into your life through the Holy Spirit into your heart (Revelation 3:20).
  • Yesu yageze mu bungingo bwaew (Ibyahishuwe 3:20)

  • Ibyaha byawe byababaliwe (Abakolosai 1:13)
  • You became a child of God (John 1:12)

  • You began the great adventure for which God created you. (John 10:10; 2 Corinthians 5:17; 1 Thessalonians 5:18)
  • Uzarushaho kumenya iby’lmana (Yahana 10:10; 2 Abakorinto 5:14-17)
  • Can you think of anything more wonderful that could happen to you than receiving Christ?
  • Mbese utekereza ko hari ikindi kintu cyakunezeza kuruta kwakira Yesu mu bugingo bwawe?

SUGGESTIONS FOR CHRISTIAN GROWTH UBURYO BWO GUKURIRA MU MWUKA
Spiritual growth results from trusting Jesus Christ. "...The righteous man shall live by faith" (Galatians 3:11). A life of faith will enable you to trust God increasingly with every detail of your life, and to practice the following: Nk’uko urnhinja rugomba gukura, ni ko nawe ugomba gukura mu mibereho y’Umwuka. Ibi bikurikira bizagufasha kugira imibereho myiza n’ubikurikiza buri munsi.
  • Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7)
  • Jya uvugana n’lmana mu buryo bwo gusenga (Yohana 15:7).
  • Read God's Word daily. (see Acts 17:11) Begin with the Gospel of John.
  • Jya usoma Ijambo ry’lmana (Ibyakozwe n’lntumwa 17:11).
  • Obey God moment by moment. (see John 14:21)
  • Ube umwizerwa imbere y’lmana (Yohana 14:21)
  • Witness for Christ by your life and words. (see Matthew 4:19 and John 15:8)
  • Iby’ukora n’iby’uvuga bijye bihamya Kristo (Matayo 4:19; Yohana 15:8)
  • Trust God for every detail of your life (1 Peter 5:7).
  • Wilingire Imana mu mibereho yawe yose (I Petero 5:7)
  • Holy Spirit – allow Him to control and empower your daily life and witness (Galatians 5:16,17; Acts 1:8).
  • Ujye ureka Umwuka Wera agutegeke, akuyobore kandi agushoboze (Abagalatia 5:16-18; Ibyakorwe n’lntumwa 1:8).
FELLOWSHIP IN A GOOD CHURCH ABAKRISTO BAGOMBA GUTERANIA HAMWE KUKO ALIBYO IGIRAKAMATRO
The Bible tells us the importance of meeting together with other Christians (Hebrews 10:25).
Have you ever watched a fire burning in a fireplace? Several logs together burn brightly. But if you pull one log away from the fire, its flame soon goes out. The same thing happens to us if we do not spend time with other Christians. Attend a church where Christ is talked about and the Bible is taught. Start this week and make plans to attend regularly. 		Biblia itubwira ko dukwillye auteranira hamwe (Abaheburayo 10:25). Kugira nao tubyumve, reka tuvuae ku by’umurfro nkticyiteaerezo: Iyo inkwi zicaniwe hamwe zirushaho kwaka neza, aliko ukuyemo urukwi rumwe ukarushyira ukwarwo, rurazima. Uko ni ko nawe udashobora kuaira imibereho ya gikristo udafatanije n’abandi. Nufatanya n’abandi bamaze kwakira Yesu koba Umukiza wabo, ni bwo uzarushaho kuaira umuriro wo kwizera mu mutima wawe.
Start this week, and make plans to attend regularly. Ujye ufatanya ntitorero ry’abantu bizera ko Ijambo ry’lmana ari iry’ukuri kandi bahimbaza Yesu no kumwubaha.
