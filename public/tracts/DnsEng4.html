Have you heard of the Four Spiritual Laws? Har du hørt om de fire åndelige love ?
Just as there are physical laws that govern the physical universe, so are there spiritual laws that govern your relationship with God. Ligesom der er naturlove, der styrer universet, på samme måde er der åndelige love, som bestemmer dit forhold til Gud.
1

LAW ONE

God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

1

LOV NR. 1

Gud elsker dig og Han har en vidunderlig plan for dit liv.

(References contained in this booklet should be read in context from the Bible whenever possible) (De anførte Bibelcitater bør så vidt muligt læses i deres bibelske sammenhæng).
God's Love Guds kærlighed
"For God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish but have eternal life." (John 3:16, NIV) “Thi således elskede Gud verden, at Han gav sin Søn den enbårne, for at enhver, som tror på Ham, ikke skal fortabes, men have evigt liv” (Johannes 3:16).
God's Plan Guds plan
[Christ speaking] “I came that they might have life, and might have it abundantly” [that it might be full and meaningful]. (John 10:10) [Kristus siger:] “Jeg er kommet, for at de skal have liv og have overflod” (dvs. et liv, der er rigt og fuldt af mening) (Johannes 10:10).
Why is it that most people are not experiencing the abundant life? Because... Hvordan kan det være, at de fleste mennesker ikke oplever dette liv i overflod?
2

LAW TWO

People are SINFUL and SEPARATED from God so we cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for our life.

2

LOV NR. 2

Mennesket er syndigt og adskilt fra gud og kan derfor ikke kende og opleve guds kærlighed og plan for sit liv.

People are Sinful Mennesket er syndigt
"All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23) “Alle har jo syndet og mangler herligheden fra Gud” (Romerne 3:23).
We were created to have a personal relationship with God, but by our own choice and self-will we have gone our own independent way and that relationship has been broken. This self-will, often seen as an attitude of active rebellion towards God or a lack of interest in Him, is an evidence of what the Bible calls sin. Mennesket blev skabt til at have samfund med Gud; men på grund af dets egen stædige selvrådighed valgte det at gå sin egen uafhængige vej og fællesskabet med Gud blev afbrudt. Denne selvrådighed, som kendetegnes af åbent oprør eller passiv ligegyldighed, er et bevis på det, som Bibelen kalder synd.
People are Separated Mennesket er adskildt fra Gud
"The wages of sin is death" [spiritual separation from God]. (Romans 6:23) “Thi syndens løn er døden” (åndelig adskillelse fra Gud) (Romerne 6:23).
This picture illustrates that God is holy and people are sinful. A great gap separates the two. The arrows illustrate that people are continually trying to reach God and the abundant life through their own efforts, such as a good life, philosophy, or religion – but they always fail. Gud er hellig og mennesket er syndigt. Et stort svælg adskiller de to fra hinanden. Mennesket prøver hele tiden på at nå Gud og livet i overflod ved egne anstrengelser: Gode gerninger, moral, filosofi osv.
The third law explains the only way to bridge this gap… Den tredje lov giver os den eneste løsning på dette dilemma...
3

LAW THREE

Jesus Christ is God’s ONLY provision for our sin. Through Him alone you can know and experience God’s love and plan for your life.

3

LOV NR. 3

Jesus Kristus er Guds eneste udvej for menneskets synd. Gennem Ham kan du både kende og opleve Guds kęrlighed og plan for dit liv.

He Died in Our Place Han døde i vort sted.
"God demonstrates His own love toward us, in that while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us." (Romans 5:8) “Men Gud viser sin kærlighed mod os ved, at Kristus døde for os, mens vi endnu var syndere” (Romerne 5:8).
He Rose From the Dead Han opstod fra de døde.
"Christ died for our sins…He was buried…He was raised on the third day, according to the Scriptures…He appeared to Peter, then to the Twelve. After that He appeared to more than five hundred…" (1 Corinthians 15:3-6) “Kristus døde for vore synder ... Han blev begravet ... Han er opstået på den tredje dag, efter Skrifterne ... Han blev set af Kefas, derefter af de tolv; derefter blev Han set af over fem hundrede brødre” (1 Korinter 15:3-6).
He Is the Only Way to God Han er den eneste vej.
"Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father but through Me.'" (John 14:6) "Jesus sagde: 'Jeg er vejen og sandheden og livet, ingen kommer til Faderen uden ved mig'”. (Johannes 14:6)
This diagram illustrates that God has bridged the gulf that separates us from Him by sending His Son, Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place to pay the penalty for our sins. Gud har slået bro over det svælg, som adskiller os fra Ham, ved at sende sin Søn, Jesus Kristus, for at Han skulle dø på korset i vort sted.
It is not enough just to know these three laws... Det er ikke nok blot at kende disse tre love....
4

LAW FOUR

We must individually RECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

4

LOV NR. 4

Hver enkelt af os må tage imod Jesus Kristus som Frelser og Herre; så kan vi kende og opleve Guds kærlighed og plan for vort liv.

We Must Receive Christ Vi må tage imod Kristus
"As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, even to those who believe in His name." (John 1:12) “Men alle dem, som tog imod Ham, gav Han magt til at blive Guds børn, dem, som tror på Hans navn” (Johannes 1:12).
We Receive Christ Through Faith Vi tager imod Kristus ved tro
"By grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God; not as a result of works so that no one may boast." (Ephesians 2:8,9) “Thi af nåden er I frelst ved tro; det skyldes ikke jer selv, Guds er gaven; det skyldes ikke gerninger, så nogen kan rose sig” (Efeserne 2:8-9).
We Receive Christ by Personal Invitation Vi tager imod Kristus ved en personlig indbydelse
[Christ is speaking] “Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if any one hears My voice and opens the door, I will come in to him.” (Revelation 3:20) [Kristus siger:] “Se, jeg står for døren og banker; om nogen hører min røst og åbner døren, da vil jeg gå ind til ham” (Åb.3:20).
Receiving Christ involves turning to God from self (repentance) and trusting Christ to come into our lives to forgive our sins and to make us what He wants us to be. Just to agree intellectually that Jesus Christ is the Son of God and that He died on the cross for our sins is not enough. Nor is it enough to have an emotional experience. We receive Jesus Christ by faith, as an act of the will. At tage imod Kristus vil sige, at vi vender os til Gud fra selvet (omvendelse) og stoler på, at Kristus kommer ind i vort liv, tilgiver os vore synder og gør os til den, hvad Han ønsker vi skal være. At acceptere forstandsmæssigt at Jesus Kristus er Guds søn og at han døde på korset for vores synder, er ikke nok. Det er heller ikke nok at have en følelsesmæssig oplevelse. Vi tager imod Kristus i tro, som en beslutning med vores vilje .

You Can Receive Christ Right Now by Faith Through Prayer Du kan tage imod Kristus i dette øjeblik ved bøn.
(Prayer is talking with God) (Bøn er samtale med Gud).
God knows your heart and is not so concerned with your words as He is with the attitude of your heart. The following is a suggested prayer: Gud kender dit hjerte og lægger ikke så stor vægt på dine ord som på dit hjertes indstilling. Du kan f. eks bede sådan:
Lord Jesus, I need You. Thank you for dying on the cross for my sins. I open the door of my life and receive You as my Savior and Lord. Thank You for forgiving my sins and giving me eternal life. Take control of the throne of my life. Make me the kind of person You want me to be. “Kære Herre Jesus! Jeg behøver dig. Tak fordi du døde på korset i mit sted for mine synder. Jeg åbner mit hjertes dør og modtager dig som min Frelser og Herre. Tak fordi du tilgiver mine synder og giver mig evigt liv. Sæt dig på mit livs trone. Gør mig til den person, du ønsker jeg skal være. ”
Does this prayer express the desire of your heart? If it does, I invite you to pray this prayer right now. Udtrykker denne bøn dit hjertes ønske?
Christ will come into your life as He promised. Hvis den gør det, så bed denne bøn i dette øjeblik og Kristus vil komme ind i dit liv, sådan som Han har lovet det i sit Ord.
How to Know That Christ Is in Your Life Hvordan kan du vide, at Kristus er i dit liv?
Did you receive Christ into your life? According to His promise in Revelation 3:20, where is Christ right now in relation to you? Christ said that He would come into your life. Would He mislead you? On what authority do you know that God has answered your prayer? (The trustworthiness of God Himself and His Word, the Bible.) Har du modtaget Kristus i dit liv? Ifølge Hans løfte i Åb. 3:20, hvor er Kristus nu i dette øjeblik i forhold til dig? Kristus har sagt, at Han vil komme ind i dit liv. Mon Han vil vildlede dig? Ud fra hvilken autoritet kan du vide, at Gud har besvaret din bøn? (Guds troværdighed og hans ord, bibelen.)
The Bible promises Eternal Life to all who receive Christ. Bibelen lover evigt liv til alle, som tager imod Kristus.
"God has given us eternal life, and this life is in His Son. He who has the Son has the life; he who does not have the Son of God does not have the life. These things I have written to you who believe in the name of the Son of God, so that you may know that you have eternal life." (1 John 5:11-13) “Og dette er vidnesbyrdet, at Gud har givet os evigt liv; og det liv er i Hans Søn. Den, som har Sønnen, har livet; den, som ikke har Guds søn, har ikke livet. Dette har jeg skrevet til jer, for at I skal vide, at I har evigt liv, I som tror på Guds Søns navn” (1 Johannes 5:11-13).
Thank God often that Christ is in your life and that He will never leave you (Hebrews 13:5). You can know on the basis of His promise that Christ lives in you and that you have eternal life from the very moment you invite Him in. He will not deceive you. Tak ofte Gud for, at Kristus er i dit liv, og at Han aldrig vil forlade dig (Hebræerne 13:5). Du kan vide, at den levende Kristus bor i dig, og at du har evigt liv, lige fra det øjeblik du indbyder Ham til at komme ind i dit liv - på grundlag af Hans løfte. Han vil ikke bedrage dig!
Do Not rely on your Feelings Vær ikke afhængig af dine følelser!
The promise of God's Word, the Bible - not our feelings - is our authority. The Christian lives by faith (trust) in the trustworthiness of God Himself and His Word. This train diagram illustrates the relationship among fact (God and His Word), faith (our trust in God and His Word), and feeling (the result of our faith and obedience). (Read John 14:21) Guds Ords løfter - ikke vore følelser - er vor autoritet. En kristen lever ved tro (tillid) til Guds troværdighed og til Hans ords troværdighed. Toget nedenfor viser forholdet mellem faktum (Gud og Hans Ord), tro (vor tillid til Gud og Hans Ord) og følelser (resultatet af vor tro og lydighed) (Johannes 14:21).
The train will run with or without a passenger car. However, it would be useless to attempt to pull the train by the passenger car. In the same way, as Christians we do not depend on feelings or emotions to decide what is true, but we place our faith (trust) in the trustworthiness of God and the promises of His Word. Toget kan køre både med og uden personvogn. Det ville imidlertid være meningsløst at lade personvognen trække toget. På samme måde bør vi kristne ikke være afhængige af vore følelser, men sætte vor tro (tillid) til Guds troværdighed og til løfterne i Hans Ord.
Now That You Have Received Christ Når du nu har taget imod Kristus
The moment you received Christ by faith, as an act of the will, many things happened, including the following: I det øjeblik du modtog Kristus i tro skete der mange ting, blandt andet følgende:
  • Christ came into your life (Revelation 3:20 and Colossians 1:27)
  • Kristus kom ind i dit liv (Åb. 3:20 og Kol. 1:27).
  • Your sins were forgiven (Colossians 1:14)
  • Dine synder blev tilgivet (Kol. 1:14).
  • You became a child of God (John 1:12)
  • Du blev et Guds barn (Joh. 1:12).
  • You received eternal life (John 5:24)
  • Du modtog evigt liv. (Joh. 5:24)
  • You began the great adventure for which God created you. (John 10:10; 2 Corinthians 5:17; 1 Thessalonians 5:18)
  • Du begyndte på det store eventyr, som Gud havde skabt dig til. (Joh. 10:10; 2 Kor. 5:17 og 1 Tes. 5:18).
Can you think of anything more wonderful that could happen to you than receiving Christ? Would you like to thank God in prayer right now for what He has done for you? By thanking God, you demonstrate your faith. Kan du forestille dig noget mere vidunderligt, end at tage imod Kristus? Vil du ikke gerne takke Gud nu, for det Han har gjort for dig? Selve denne handling, at takke Gud, er et udtryk for tro.
Now what? Hvad nu?
Suggestions for Christian Growth Forslag til Kristen Vækst
Spiritual growth results from trusting Jesus Christ. "...The righteous man shall live by faith" (Galatians 3:11). A life of faith will enable you to trust God increasingly with every detail of your life, and to practice the following: Åndelig vækst er et resultat af, at sætte sin lid til Jesus Kristus. "Den retfærdige skal leve af tro" (Gal. 3:11). Et liv i tro, vil gøre det muligt for dig i stadig højere grad at betro Gud med hver eneste detalje af dit liv og at praktisere følgende:
  • Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7)
  • Tal med Gud i bøn daglig (Joh. 15:7).
  • Read God's Word daily. (see Acts 17:11) Begin with the Gospel of John.
  • Læs Guds Ord daglig (Ap.g. 17:11) - begynd med Johannesevangeliet.
  • Obey God moment by moment. (see John 14:21)
  • Adlyd Gud - øjeblik for øjeblik (Joh. 14:21).
  • Witness for Christ by your life and words. (see Matthew 4:19 and John 15:8)
  • Vær et vidne for Kristus i liv og ord (Mat. 4:19; Joh. 15:8).
  • Trust God for every detail of your life. (1 Peter 5:7)
  • Stol på Gud i hver eneste detalje af dit liv (1 Pet. 5:7).
  • Holy Spirit – allow Him to control and empower your daily life and witness (Galatians 5:16,17; Acts 1:8).
  • Lad Helligånden kontrollere og udruste dig i dit daglige liv og vidnesbyrd (Gal. 5:16-17; Ap.g. 1:8).
Fellowship in a Good Church Betydningen af en god kirke eller menighed.
The Bible tells us the importance of meeting together with other Christians (Hebrews 10:25).
Have you ever watched a fire burning in a fireplace? Several logs together burn brightly. But if you pull one log away from the fire, its flame soon goes out. The same thing happens to us if we do not spend time with other Christians. Attend a church where Christ is talked about and the Bible is taught. Start this week and make plans to attend regularly. 		I Hebręerbrevet 10:25 stilles vi over for fųlgende formaning: “Lad os ikke svigte vor egen menighedsforssamling.” Mange bręndeknuder bręnder udmęrket sammen; men hvis man “trękker et stykke bręnde ud af ilden og lęgger det på pladen foran ovnen, vil ilden hurtigt slukkes. Sådan er det også i vort forhold til andre kristne. Hvis du lkke allerede hųrer til i en kirke eller menighed, skal du ikke vente på en opfordring til at tilslutte dig den. Tag selv initativet; besųg eller ring til pręsten eller forstanderen for den nęrmeste kirke eller menighed, hvor Kristus bliver herliggjort, og Hans Ord bliver forkyndt.
