Have you heard of the Four Spiritual Laws? Ko Ka Taba Jin Labarin Ka’idodi Hudu Na Ruhaniya ?
Just as there are physical laws that govern the physical universe, so are there spiritual laws that govern your relationship with God. Kamar yadda mu ke da dokoki a kasarmu domin zaman lafiya da salama, haka nan Allah yana da ka’idodin da dole mu san su idan muna bukatar zaman lafiya da shi.
1

God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

1

Allah yana KAUNARKA kuma yana mika maka kyakkyawan SHIRI don rayuwarka.

God's Love GAME DA KAUNAR ALLAH
“God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish, but have eternal life” (John 3:16 NIV). “Gama Allah ya kaunaci duniya har ya ba da makadaicin Dansa don dukan wanda ke dogara gare shi kada ya halaka, amma ya sami rai madawwami.” (Yahaya 3:16)
God's Plan GAME DA SHIRIN ALLAH:
Jesus said, "I came that they may have life, and have it abundantly" [that it might be full and meaningful]." (John 10:10) [Kristi ya ce] “Dalilin zuwana ke nan in ba da rai a yalwace.” (Yahaya 10:10)
Why is it that most people are not experiencing the abundant life? Because... Amma mutane da yawa ba sa jin dadm rayuwarsu domin…
2

People are SINFUL and SEPARATED from God so we cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for our life.

2

Mutum yana da ZUNUBI, kuma ya rabu da allah, don haka ba ya iya sanin kaunar allah da shirin nan na rayuwarsa.

People are Sinful MUTUM MAI ZUNUBI NE:
"All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23) “I, kowa ya yi zunubi, kowa ya kasa ga darajar Allah.” (Romawa 3:23)
Man was created to have fellowship with God; but, because of his stubborn self-will, he chose to go his own independent way, and fellowship with God was broken. This self-will, characterized by an attitude of active rebellion or passive indifference, is an evidence of what the Bible calls sin. "We all, like sheep have gone astray, each of us has turned to his own way;" (isa 53:6) Allah ya halici mutum domin yana so ya yi zumunta tare da shi. Amma mutum ya zabi bin nufinkansa, saboda haka zaman lafiya da Allah ya gagare shi. Bin nufin kanmu cikin kowane abu,babba ko karami, shi ne Littafi Mai Tsarki yake kira zunubi. “Dukanmu kamar tumaki muka bata, kowa yana bin nasa tafarki.” (Ishaya 53:6)
People are Separated MUTUM YA RABU DA ALLAH:
"The wages of sin is death" [spiritual separation from God]. (Romans 6:23) “Gama hakin zunubi mutuwa ne [rabuwa da Allah shi ne mutuwa ta ruhaniya.”] (Romawa 6:23)
This picture illustrates that God is holy and people are sinful. A great gap separates the two. The arrows illustrate that people are continually trying to reach God and the abundant life through their own efforts, such as a good life, philosophy, or religion – but they always fail. Wannan zane yana nuna yadda Allah Mai Tsarki ba ya zama tare da mutum mai zunubi. Kibawun nan suna nuna yadda mutum yana neman zuwa wurin Allah ta kokarin kansa, alal misali. Ta hanyoyin addini, da zaman aminci, da addu’a, da taron sujada da dai sauransu. Amma wadannan duka ba za su kai mutum ga ceto ba.
The third law explains the only way to bridge this gulf… Ka’idan nan ta uku ce kadai ta ke nuna mana hanya daya tak wadda za mu bi don mu koma wurin Allah
3

Jesus Christ is God’s ONLY provision for our sin. Through Him alone you can know and experience God’s love and plan for your life.

3

Ba wata hanya da za ta kai mu wurin Allah sal ta wurin Yesu almasihu. Ya mutu dominmu. Ta wurinsa kadai za ka iya sanin kaunar Allah da shirinda ya yi domin rayuwarka.

HE DIED IN OUR PLACE ALMASIHU TA MUTU DOMINMU:
“God demonstrates His own love toward us, in that while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us” (Romans 5:8). “Allah ya nuna kaunarsa zuwa gare mu yadda Kristi ya mutu dominmu. tun muna cikin zunubi.” (Romawa 5:8)
HE ROSE FROM THE DEAD ALMASIHU YA TASHI DAGA CIKIN MATATTU:
"Christ died for our sins…He was buried…He was raised on the third day, according to the Scriptures…He appeared to Peter, then to the Twelve. After that He appeared to more than five hundred…" (1 Corinthians 15:3-6) Älmashiu ya mutu domin zunubanmu ... an binne shi...kuma an ta da shi a rana ta uku. kamar yadda annabawa suka yi annabci.” (I Korantiyawa 15:3-4)
He Is the Only Way to God BAN DA YESU BABU HANYAR CETO:
"Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father but through Me.'" (John 14:6) Yesu ya ce masa, Ni ne hanya. Ni ne gaskiya. Ni ne rai; ba mai zuwa wurin Uba sai ta wurina” (Yahaya 14:6)
This diagram illustrates that God has bridged the gulf that separates us from Him by sending His Son, Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place to pay the penalty for our sins. Wannan zane yana nuna yadda Allah shi kadai zai jawo mu zuwa ga kansa. Ya aikata wannan sa’ad da ya aiko Yesu domin ya mutu bias gicciye saboda zunubanmu.
It is not enough just to know these three laws... Amma sanin ka’idodin nan uku kurum bai isa ba…
4

We must individually RECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

4

Dole kowannenmu ya KARBI Yesu Kristi ya zama mai cetonsa da ubangijinsa; ta haka kadaiza mu sha dadin kaunar Allah da shirin da ya yl don rayuwarmu.

WE MUST RECEIVE CHRIST DOLE MU KARBI YESU TA WURIN BANGASKIYA:
"As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, even to those who believe in His name." (John 1:12) “Amma dukan wadanda suka karbe shi, ya ba su iko su zama ‘ya’yan Allah ta wurin ba da gaskiya ga sunansa.” (Yahaya 1:12)
"By grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God; not as a result of works so that no one may boast." (Ephesians 2:8,9) ”Sabode alherinsa ne an cece ku ta wurin bangaskiya cikin Kristi ... Ceto ba bisa kokarin kanmu bane, don haka ba mai iya yin fahariya.” (Afisawa 2:8,9)
We Receive Christ by Personal Invitation IN ZA MU KARBI YESU DOLE MU GAYYACE SHI:
[Christ speaking] "Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if anyone hears My voice and opens the door, I will come in to him." (Revelation 3:20) Yesu ya ce. “Gama ina tsaye a bakin kofa (In zuciyarka) ina kwankwasawa; duka wanda ya ji muryata, kuma ya bude kofa zan shiga wurinsa.” (Wahayin Yahaya 3:20)
Receiving Christ involves turning to God from self (repentance) and trusting Christ to come into our lives to forgive our sins and to make us what He wants us to be. Just to agree intellectually that Jesus Christ is the Son of God and that He died on the cross for our sins is not enough. Nor is it enough to have an emotional experience. We receive Jesus Christ by faith, as an act of the will. Karbar Yesu ya shafi gaskatawa cewa Allah zai shiga zukatanmu ya gafarta mana zunubanmu. Dole ne mu juya daga zunubi, mu ba Yesu dama ya yi zamansa a cikinmu.

Duk wadannan suna karkashin nufin mutum. Duk wadannan suna karkashin nufin mutu. Don haka mutumin nan ba shi da salama. ‎(Self is on the throne. Christ is outside the life. Interests are directed by self, often resulting in discord and frustration.)‎

Self is yielding to Christ. Interests are directed by Christ, resulting in harmony with God's plan.)‎

Allah ya san zuciyarka, kuma ba ka iyarudinsa ba, domin haka ka ba da gaskiya cikin addu’arka.

Take control of the throne of my life. Make me the kind of person You want me to be. “Ya Ubangiji Yesu, ta wurin bin nufin kaina na yi maka zunubi. Ka shigo zuciyata, ka gafartamini zunubaina. Ka yi mulki bisa kaina, ka maishe ni sabon mutum. Na gode domin ka amsaaddu’ata. Amin"
Does this prayer express the desire of your heart? If it does, I invite you to pray this prayer right now. Kana so ka yi wannan addu’a?

KADA KA DOGARA GA YADDA KA KE JI
Thank God often that Christ is in your life. He will never leave you (Hebrews 13:5). You can know that Christ lives in you and that you have eternal life because of His promise. Yau kana muma, mai yiwuwa ne gobe za ka ji dan bakin ciki. Ko da yaya ka ke ji, Yesu Kristiyana nan cikinzuciyarka, don ya ce, “Ba zan bar ka kai kadai ba.” (Ibraniyawa 13:5) Ka dogaraga Maganar (alkawaran) Allah. Kada ka dogara ga yadda ka ke ji.
NOW THAT YOU HAVE RECEIVED CHRIST SA’AD DA KA KARBI YESU KRISTI, ABUBUWA DA YAWA ZA SUFARU…
The moment you received Christ by faith, as an act of the will, many things happened, including the following: Ga wadansu:
  • Christ came into your life (Revelation 3:20 and Colossians 1:27)
  • I. Yesu Kristi ya shiga cikin zuciyarka da zamanka. (Wahayin. Yahaya 3:20)
  • Your sins were forgiven (Colossians 1:14)
  • An gafarta maka zunubanka (Kolosiyawa 1:14)
  • You became a child of God (John 1:12)
  • An maishe ka daya daga cikin ‘ya’ yan Allah. (Yahaya 1:12)
  • You began the great adventure for which God created you. (John 10:10; 2 Corinthians 5:17; 1 Thessalonians 5:18)
  • Kana da rai na har abada. (Yahaya 5:24)
Can you think of anything more wonderful that could happen to you than receiving Christ? Would you like to thank God in prayer right now for what He has done for you? By thanking God, you demonstrate your faith. 5. Ka fara jin dadin zamanka domin Kristi yana rayuwa cikinka. (Yahaya 10:10; 2 Korantiyawa 5:17)
Now what? Wadan nan abubuwa da alkawarai da dama daga wurin Allah naka ne. Ka karanta su daga cikin Littafi Mai Tsarki. Cikin Ayyukan Manzanni 17:11 wadansu mutane suka rika bincike Maganar Allah kowace rana, domin su gane ko gaskiya ce.
SUGGESTIONS FOR CHRISTIAN GROWTH SHA WARWARI A KAN YADDA ZA KA YL GIRMA CIKIN KRISTI:
  • Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7)
  • Ka yi addu’a ga Allah ko wace rana. (Luka 18:1)
  • Read God’s Word daily (Psalm 119:11); begin with the Gospel of John.
  • Ka karanta maganar Allah ko wace rana. (Zabura 119:11) Ka fara karatu a Bishara ta hannun Yahaya.
  • Obey God moment by moment. (see John 14:21)
  • Ka yi biyayya ga Allah kullum. (Yahaya 14:21)
  • Witness for Christ by your life and words. (see Matthew 4:19 and John 15:8)
  • Ka shaida Kristi ta wurin zamanka da kuma maganarka. (Luka 8:39)
  • Trust God for every detail of your life (1 Peter 5:7).
  • Ka dogara ga Allah cikin ko wane abu. (1 Bitrus 5:7)
  • Holy Spirit – allow Him to control and empower your daily life and witness (Galatians 5:16,17; Acts 1:8).
  • Ka yarda Ruhu Mai Tsarki wanda ke tare da kai yanzu, ya ba ka ikon yin kome a dukan zamanka ko wace rana. (Galatiyawa 5:16,17; Ayyukan Manzanni 1:8)
