YOU CAN ACCEPT CHRIST RIGHT NOW THROUGH A PRAYER OF FAITH. TUS NTSEEG ZAJ LUS THOV
(Prayer is talking with God.) (Kev thov Vajtswv yog nrog Vajtswv sib tham)
God knows what you want to say and if you truly mean it. Vajtswv yeej paub tej uas koj xav hais thiab saib koj puas hais tseeb tiag.
THIS IS THE PRAYER YOU CAN USE. KOJ SIV ZAJ LUS THOV NO LOS TAU.
‘Jesus, I need you in my life. I believe in you as my Saviour and Lord. Thank you for forgiving my sins and giving me eternal life. Take control of my life. Make me the kind of person you want me to be. Amen’ ‘Tswv Yexus, kuv xav tau koj los nyob hauv kuv lub neej. Kuv ntseeg Koj ua kuv tus Cawmseej thiab tus Tswv. Ua tsaug Koj tau zam kuv tej kev txhaum thiab muab txojsa ntev rau kuv. Thov Koj tswj kuv lub neej. Thov Koj hloov kuv ua tus tibneeg uas koj xav kom kuv ua. Asmee.
Is this prayer what you would like to say to God? Zaj lus thov no puas yog zaj koj xav hais rau Vajtswv?
If it is, would you like to pray this prayer right now? Yog haistias yog, koj puas kam hais zaj lus thov no tamsim no?
WHAT JESUS HAS DONE FOR YOU TEJ YEXUS TAU UA RAU KOJ
When you believe in Jesus as your Saviour and Lord you can be sure that: Thaum koj ntseeg Yexus ua koj tus Cawmseej thiab tus Tswv, koj yeej paub tseeb haistias:
  1. Jesus came into your life. (Revelations 3:30, Colossians 1:27)
  2. Your sins were forgiven. (Colossians 1:14)
  3. You became a child of God. (John 1:12)
  4. You have eternal life. (John 5:24)
  5. You began the new life for which God made you. (John 10:10)
  1. Khetos los rau hauv koj lub neej (Tshwmsim 3:30; Khaulauxais 1:27)
  2. Vajtswv tau zam koj tej kev txhaum lawm (Khaulauxais 1:14)
  3. Koj yog Vajtswv ib tug menyuam (Yauhas 1:12)
  4. Koj tau txojsia ntev (Yauhas 5:24)
  5. Koj pib ua lub neej tshiab uas Vajtswv coj koj mus ua (Yauhas 10:10)
NOW YOU SHOULD: ZIAG NO KOJ YUAV TAU:
Go to a church where Jesus Christ is truly worshipped and where God’s Word (the Bible) is taught. Mus koom ib pawg-ntseeg uas hawm Yexus Khetos thiab qhia Vajtswv Txojlus tseeb.
Read God’s Word daily or find someone who will read it to you. Koj nyeem Vajtswv Txojlus txhua hnub losyog nrhiav ib tus tibneeg nyeem rau koj.
