Have you heard of the Four Spiritual Laws? Xana Utwile Hita Milawu Ya Mune Ya Moya Ke?
Just as there are physical laws that govern the physical universe, so are there spiritual laws that govern your relationship with God. Kuni milawu ya ntumbuluko leyi fumaka misava leyi hihanyaka e ka yona. Switano kuni milawu ya Moya leyi fumaka vunakulobye bya wena na Xikwembu.
1

God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

1

Xikwembu XAKURANDZA, kutani xiku lulamisele KUNGU rohlamarisa evutomini bya wena.

God's Love RIRHANDZU RA XIKWEMBU
“God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish, but have eternal life” (John 3:16 NIV). Hikuva Xikwembu Xirandzile misava swo nghasi xiko xinyika nwana wa xona langa tswariwa ariyexe, leswaku unwana ni unwana lapfumeleka eka yena anga tshuki a lova kambe avani vutomi lebyi nga heriki. (John 3:16)
God's Plan KUNGU RA XIKWEMBU
Jesus said, "I came that they may have life, and have it abundantly" [that it might be full and meaningful]." (John 10:10) (Kriste ute) Ndzitile leswaku vakuma vutomi vabylkuma hi ntalo. (John 10:10).
Why is it that most people are not experiencing the abundant life? Because... Ahivanhu hinkwavo lava hanyaka vutomi lebyi, hikwalahoka…
2

People are SINFUL and SEPARATED from God so we cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for our life.

2

Munhu UDYOHILE, xidyoho XINWIHAMBANISILE na xikwembu, kutani angaswikoti kutiva nl ku kutwisisa rirhandzu ra xikwembu na kungu ra xona evutomini bya yena.

People are Sinful MUNHU UDYOHILE
"All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23) Hikuva hikwavo va dyohile, kutani a va faneriwi hiku kwetsima ka Xikwembu. (vaRoma 3:23)
We were created to have a personal relationship with God, but by our own choice and self-will we have gone our own independent way and that relationship has been broken. This self-will, often seen as an attitude of active rebellion towards God or a lack of interest in Him, is an evidence of what the Bible calls sin. Munhu a a vumberiwe kuva na vunakulobye na Xikwembu, kambe munhu atihlawulela kufamba hindlela ya yena, kutani vunakulobye na Xikwoembu byi onhaka. Leswi Bibele yi swivula ka vudyoho. Swikombisiwa hindlela leyi (1) munhu ulwa na Xikwembu hikutiarisa (2) kungavi na mhaka na Xikwembu (3) nakambe munhu angahanyi vutomi lebyi lulameke hiku lerisa ka Xikwembu.
People are Separated MUNHU U HAMBANILE NA XIKWEMBU
"The wages of sin is death" [spiritual separation from God]. (Romans 6:23) Hikuva hakelo ya vudyoho irifu. (kuhambana ka moya na, Xikwembu) (vaRoma 6:23)
This picture illustrates that God is holy and people are sinful. A great gap separates the two. The arrows illustrate that people are continually trying to reach God and the abundant life through their own efforts, such as a good life, philosophy, or religion – but they always fail. Xikwembu xakwetsima kasi munhu imudyohi. Kutani kuni kuhambana lokukulu exhikari ka vona ha vambiri. Kutani miseve leyi yi kombisa hilaha munhu aringetaka ku kuma vutomi bya ntalo hi matshalatshala yakwe, hikuhanya vutomi lebyinene, vukhongeri, na mitirho yo pfuna vanwana, kambe matshalatshala ya yena a ya pfuni nchumu.
The third law explains the only way to bridge this gulf… Nawu wa vunharhu hiwona wuri woxe lowu hi hlamulelaka xirhalanganya lexi...
3

Jesus Christ is God’s ONLY provision for our sin. Through Him alone you can know and experience God’s love and plan for your life.

3

Yesu Kriste hiyena ndlela NTSENA leyi munhu anga. Swikotaka kufika eka xikwembu ha yona. Kutani ha yena hinga tiva na ku twisisa rirhandzu ra xikwembu na kungu ra xona evutominibya hlna.

HE DIED IN OUR PLACE UFILE A TLHELA A PFUKA EXIKARHI KAVAFI
“For Christ died for sins once for all, the righteous for the unrighteous, to bring you to God.” (1Peter 3:18) Hikuva na yena Kriste o twisiwile ku vava, kanwe hikwalaho ka swidyoho, yena la lulameke ematshanweni yalavo homboloka leswaku ativa kuhitisa e ka Xikwembu. (1 Petre 3:18)
“After his suffering, he showed himself to these men and gave many convincing proofs that he was alive. He appeared to them over a period of forty days and spoke about the kingdom of God.” (Acts 1:3) Vangalavo loko a twisiwe ku vaviseka angati komba eka vona a hanva hiswikombiso leswo tala leswikhorwisaka va nwi vona himasiku ya mune wa makume arikarhi avula ta ku fuma ka Xikwembu. (Timhaka 1:3)
He Is the Only Way to God HL YENA NDLALA ARI YEXE
"Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father but through Me.'" (John 14:6) Yesu aku kayena Himina ndlela ni ntiyiso ni vutomi aku na munhu la nga taka ka Tatana loko ku ngari ha mina. (John 14:6)
This diagram illustrates that God has bridged the gulf that separates us from Him by sending His Son, Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place to pay the penalty for our sins. Xikwembu xi endlile buloho ekheleni leri ngiri hi hambanise na Xona hiku rhumela nwana wa Xona, Yesu Kriste kuta hi fela e Xihambanweni.
It is not enough just to know these three laws... A swiringananga kutiva ntsena milawu leyi minharhu…

We must individually RECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

4

Hifanele leswaku hl unwe unwe hl AMUKELA Yesu Kriste ava Hosi nl muponisi wa hina. Hl kona hingata tiva rirhandzu ra Xikwembu na ku twisisa kungu ra xona evutomini bya Hina.

WE MUST RECEIVE CHRIST HIFANELE KU AMUKELA KRISTE HIKUPFUMELA
"As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, even to those who believe in His name." (John 1:12) Kambe hikwavo lava nganwi amukela, lava tshembeke vito ra yena uvanyikile matimba ya ku endliwa vana va Xikwombu va nga lava pfumeleke hi vito yena. (Yohana 1:12)
"By grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God; not as a result of works so that no one may boast." (Ephesians 2:8,9) Hikuva mihlayisekile hitintswalo hiku pfumela kutani leswi aswi humi ka nwina, iku nyika ka Xikwembu, aswi humi mitirhweni leswaku kunga tshuki kuva na munhu loyi atidzunisaka. (vaEfese 2:8,9)
We Receive Christ by Personal Invitation HL AMUKELA KRISTE HIKUNWI RHAMBA LESWAKU ANGHENA E VUTOMI VYA HINA
[Christ speaking] "Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if anyone hears My voice and opens the door, I will come in to him." (Revelation 3:20) (Kriste ute) "Waswivo ndzi yimiie enyangweni ndza gongondza kungaku loko unwana ayingisa rito ra mina a pfulanyangwa, ndzita nghena eka yena ndzilalela nayena na yena adya na mina. (Hiavu 3:20)
Receiving Christ involves turning to God from self (repentance) and trusting Christ to come into our lives to forgive our sins and to make us what He wants us to be. Just to agree intellectually that Jesus Christ is the Son of God and that He died on the cross for our sins is not enough. Nor is it enough to have an emotional experience. We receive Jesus Christ by faith, as an act of the will. Aswi enelanga ku pfumelelana ntsena na tidyondzo ta Kriste ku amukela Kriste swi vula ku hundzukela eka Xikwembu kusuka vumina (kuhundzuka) Hi tshemba Kriste leswaku ute nghena evutomina bya hina ahirivalola swidyoho swa hina, hi va hi nwi pfumelela leswaku a endla hi hina leswi arandzaka ieswaku hiva swona.

The following is a suggested prayer: Nwikombele leswaku anghena evutominibya wena, tshemba leswaku uta endla leswi angatshembisa swona, yena uxiyisisa swinene leswi navelaka himbilu ya wena, ku tlula marito lawa uma vulaku hi nomu wa wena. Does this prayer express the desire of your heart? If it does, I invite you to pray this prayer right now. Xana xikhongelo lexi xivula swona leswi navelaka hi mbilu ya wena xana ke?
Christ will come into your life as He promised. Loko xi vula swona khongela sweswi kutani Kriste uta nghena evutomini bva wena tani hiloko a tshembisile.

Did you receive Christ into your life? According to His promise in Revelation 3:20, where is Christ right now in relation to you? Christ said that He would come into your life. Would He mislead you? On what authority do you know that God has answered your prayer? (The trustworthiness of Xana unwi amukerile Kriste evutomini bya wena xana? Hiku tshembisa kayena Kriste. Ukwihi evutomini bya wena tani hi Hlavutelo 3:20 Kriste ute utata nghens evutomini bya wena. Xana anga ku xisa ke? Uswitivisa kuyini leswaku Xikwembu xi hiamurile xikhongelo xa wena xana? The Bible promises Eternal Life to all who receive Christ. BIBELE YL TSHEMBISA VUTOMI LEBYI NGA HERIKI. EKA HINKWAVO LA VA AMUKELAKA KRISTE
"God has given us eternal life, and this life is in His Son. He who has the Son has the life; he who does not have the Son of God does not have the life. These things I have written to you who believe in the name of the Son of God, so that you may know that you have eternal life." (1 John 5:11-13) Xikwembu xihinyikile vutomi lebyi nga heriki. Kutani vutomi byebyo byile ka Nwana was xona. La ngani nwana unavutomi langa riki na nwana wa Xikwembu angana vutomi. Ndzi mitsalerile ti mhaka teto nwina la'va pfumelaka eka vito ra nwana wa Xikwembu mita tiva leswaku mini vutomi lebyi nga heriki. (I John 5:11-13)
Thank God often that Christ is in your life and that He will never leave you (Hebrews 13:5). You can know on the basis of His promise that Christ lives in you and that you have eternal life from the very moment you invite Him in. He will not deceive you. Nkhensa Xikwembu mikarhi hinkwayo hikuva Kriste ule vutomini bya wena. Uvurile leswaku angaka angaka tshiki (Vaheberu 13:5). Kutani unga swi tivisisa leswaku Kriste, ule vutomini bya wena, na leswaku unabyo vutorni lebyi ngaheriki hiko kwalaho ka xitshembiso xa yena.

NOW THAT YOU HAVE RECEIVED CHRIST TANI HL LESWI U AMUKELEKE KRISTE
The moment you received Christ by faith, as an act of the will, many things happened, including the following: Hinkarhi lowu unga amukela Kriste kuhumele swo tala e ka wena.
  • Christ came into your life (Revelation 3:20 and Colossians 1:27)
  • Kriste unghenile e vutomini bya wena. (Hiavutelo 3:20 na Vakolosa 1:27)
  • Your sins were forgiven (Colossians 1:14)
  • Swidyoho swa wena swirivaleriwile. (Vakolose 1:14)
  • You became a child of God (John 1:12)
  • Usungule kuva nwana wa Xikwembu. (John 1:12)
  • You began the great adventure for which God created you (John 10:10, 2 Corinthians 5:17).
  • Uta sungula ku tiva kungu ra Xikwembu na xikongomelo xa xona hi vutomi bya wena. (John 10:10 na 11 Vakorinto 5:17)
Can you think of anything more wonderful that could happen to you than receiving Christ? Would you like to thank God in prayer right now for what He has done for you? U anakanya leswaku kungava kona wu nwana nchumu wo antswa kuri niku amukela Kriste evutomini vya wena xana? Unga tsakela ku nkhensa Xikwembu hixikhongelo eka leswi xiku endleleke swona xona?
By thanking God, you demonstrate your faith. Ku nkhensa Xikwembu ku, kombisa ku pfumela ka wena ahikhongeli.
To enjoy your new life to the fullest... Kutani sweswi kuhumelela yini?
Spiritual growth results from trusting Jesus Christ. Tani hileswi unga muKriste, ufanele hiku kula e kupfumeleni ka wena.
“The righteous man shall live by faith” (Galatians 3:11). A life of faith will enable you to trust God increasingly with every detail of your life, and to practice the following: Leswi swilandzelaka swinga kupfuna ngopfu.
  • Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7)
  • Yana emahlweni ka Xikwembu hi mikhongelo masiku hinkwaho. (Yohana 15:7)
  • Read God's Word daily. (see Acts 17:11) Begin with the Gospel of John.
  • Hlaya rito ra Xikwembu masiku hinkwawo. (Timhaka 17:11) Sungula hi vangeli ya Yohana.
  • Obey God moment by moment. (see John 14:21)
  • Yingisela Xikwembu mikarhi hinkwayo. (Yohana 14:21)
  • Witness for Christ by your life and words. (see Matthew 4:19 and John 15:8)
  • Vula vumbhoni himarito na vutomi bya wena. (Matewu 4:19, Yohana 15:8)
  • Trust God for every detail of your life (1 Peter 5:7).
  • Tshemba Xikwembu e timhakeni hinkwato ta vutomi bya wena. (1 Petro 5:7)
  • Holy Spirit – allow Him to control and empower your daily life and witness (Galatians 5:16,17; Acts 1:8).
  • Pfumelela moya lowo Kwetsima leswaku wu kufuma na ku ku nyika matimba hinkwawo leswaku uvambhoni ya ntiyiso. (vaGalatiya 5:16,17 Timhaka 1:8)
FELLOWSHIP IN A GOOD CHURCH INFANELO KUYA E KEREKENI
The Bible tells us the importance of meeting together with other Christians (Hebrews 10:25).
Have you ever watched a fire burning in a fireplace? Several logs together burn brightly. But if you pull one log away from the fire, its flame soon goes out. The same thing happens to us if we do not spend time with other Christians. Attend a church where Christ is talked about and the Bible is taught. Start this week and make plans to attend regularly.
