PEOPLE ARE SINFUL CHAW YAˇ LEHˬ VENˇ BAˆUIˍ JAˇ.
‘All people have sinned.’ (They are not living the way God wants them to live.) Romans 3:23 Chaw hk'a peu-eˬ venˇ baˆte leh G'uiˬ sha ve awˬ meꞈ awˬ hponˇ htaꞈ maˇ mi taˬ . Roˇ maꞈ liꞈ 3ˇ23
People were created to be friends of God, but because we wanted to please ourselves, we chose to go our own way without God. Our friendship with God was spoiled. This going our own way means doing things that God does not want us to do or taking no interest in God. This is what the Bible calls sin. PEOPLE ARE KEPT A LONG WAY FROM GOD. CHAW YAˇ G'UIˬ SHA HTAꞈ VUIˇ K'AI VE
"The payment for sin is death." Romans 6:23 “Venˇ baˆte leh g'a ve awˬ hpfuhˇ lehˬ , suh ve teˇ ceuˬ yoˬ .” Roˇ maꞈ liꞈ 6ˇ23
This death is more than just our body dying – it means spiritual death – being kept a long way from God and not having any friendship with Him. Venˇ baˆhtaꞈ pa taw suh ve teˇ ceuˬ lehˬ awˬ to suh ve a kehˆkaꞈ jaˇ ve yoˬ - tcuhˉ kuiˬ lehˬ co ha suh ve htaꞈ k’oˆgaˇ ve yoˬ . Chaw yaˇ lehˬ yawˇ a loˍ hk'aˇ suh a shaꞈ teꞈ leh G'uiˬ sha htaꞈ awˬ chawˇ te daꞈ maˇ g’a-o.
God is holy and good while people are sinful. The difference between God and people is so great it is like a great, deep valley keeping them a long way from each other. People are always trying to reach God and find the full, best life by doing things themselves, such as trying to be good, obeying the laws of the land and being kind to others. But people can never reach God by their own effort. Chaw yaˇ venˇ baˆuiˍ jaˇ htawˇ G'uiˬ sha lehˬ daꞈ kehˬ jaˇ ve yoˬ .
