Have you heard of the Four Spiritual Laws? Nawˬ awˬ to co ha htaꞈ caˉ daꞈ ve awˬ lawn (4 ) ceuˬ htaꞈ kaˇ jaw peuˬ laˇ?

God made the sun, moon, stars and the earth.

G'uiˬ sha lehˬ mvuhˇ nyi, ha pa , meuꞈ keu teˇ hpaˍ leh miˬ guiˬ htaꞈ te hpanˍ ve yoˬ .

God made the animals, the trees, mountains, fish, birds and all the living creatures.

G'uiˬ sha lehˬ to nuˇ to shaˆ, suhˆ cehˬ hawˆ cehˬ leh, hk'aw teˇ hpaˍ, ngaˆ ehˉ teˇ hpaˍ leh a shaꞈ teꞈ ve awˬ ceuˬ hk’a peu-eˬ htaꞈ te hpanˍ ve yoˬ .

God made people, loved them, and gave them everything they needed to have a happy life.

G'uiˬ sha lehˬ chaw yaˇ htaꞈ te hpanˍ peuˬ leh Yawˇ haꞈ ve pa taw chaw yaˇ loˍ ve awˬ ceuˬ hk'a peu-eˬ piˇ ve yoˬ .

People disobeyed God and did what they wanted to do instead of what God wanted them to do.

Chaw yaˇ lehˬ Guiˬ sha hkawˇ maˇ na leh yawˇ te gaˇ ve hk’e te ve yoˬ . G'uiˬ sha yawˇ htaꞈ te tcuh gaˇ ve hk’e yawˇ maˇ te.

After people went away from God, they forgot God and began to worship things like stones, trees and evil spirits.

Chaw yaˇ lehˬ G'uiˬ sha htaꞈ vuiˇ daꞈ peuˬ hk’awꞈ nawˉ, yawˇ G'uiˬ sha htaꞈ leunˇ k’ai leh, haˉ pui haˉ hpawˇ, suhˆcehˬ hawˆcehˬ leh neˇ haiˬ sheh haiˬ htaꞈ oˉ k’oˍ taˬ pui la ve yoˬ .

Also, people had to work very hard to earn their living.

G'uiˬ sha maˇ ga piˇ ve k’o, chaw yaˇ lehˬ yawˇ hk'aˇ yawˇ kanˉ te leh a shaꞈ hu tuˬ haˍ ve yoˬ .

But God still loved people and sent His messengers to tell them the good news of His love and forgiveness.

Do you know the four things God wants you to know?

Yaꞈ hk'aˇ G'uiˬ sha lehˬ chaw yaˇ htaꞈ haꞈ chehˇ pa taw mvuhˇ naw tuch yaˇ htaꞈ hpehˇ peu leh chaw yaˇ htaꞈ Yawˇ haꞈ leh venˇ baˆhpehˇ ve awˬ lawn ha lehˬ ve bon hkawˇ ca hto tuch ve yoˬ .
