Have you heard of the Four Spiritual Laws? Koj puas tau hnov txog Sab Ntsujplig Plaub Txojcai?

God made the sun, moon, stars and the earth.

Vajtswv tsim lub hnub, lub hli, cov hnubqub thiab lub ntiajteb.

God made the animals, the trees, mountains, fish, birds and all the living creatures.

Vajtswv tsim tsiajtxhu, tej xyoob ntoo, tej roob, ntses, nasnoog thiab txhua yam tsiaj uas muaj sia.

God made people, loved them, and gave them everything they needed to have a happy life.

Vajtswv tsim tibneeg, hlub tibneeg, thiab muab txhua yam lawv yuav tsum muaj ua rau lawv tau lub neej zoo siab.

People disobeyed God and did what they wanted to do instead of what God wanted them to do.

Tibneeg tsis mloog Vajtswv lus thiab tau mus ua raws li lawv lub siab nyiam ua xwb, tsis ua raws li Vajtswv hais kom lawv ua.

After people went away from God, they forgot God and began to worship things like stones, trees and evil spirits.

Tom qab tibneeg khiav ntawm Vajtswv mus lawm, lawv tsis nco qab Vajtswv thiab pib hawm lwm yam xws li tej pobzeb, tej xyoob ntoo thiab cov ntsujplig phem.

Also, people had to work very hard to earn their living.

Thiab, tibneeg yuav tau ua dejnum hnyav heev thiaj tau noj tau haus.

But God still loved people and sent His messengers to tell them the good news of His love and forgiveness.
