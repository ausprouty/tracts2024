People are Sinful UMUNTU WONILE
"All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23) “Ngokuba bonke bonile, basilalewe inkazimulo kaNkulunkulu”. Roma 3:23
We were created to have a personal relationship with God, but by our own choice and self-will we have gone our own independent way and that relationship has been broken. This self-will, often seen as an attitude of active rebellion towards God or a lack of interest in Him, is an evidence of what the Bible calls sin. Umuntu wayedalelwe ukuba abe nobuhlobo noNkulunkulu, kodwa umuntu wazikhethela eyakhe indlela ngakho lobo buhlobo balimala. Lokhu iBhayibheli likubiza ngokuthi isoni, kubonakaliswa ukumelana noNkulunkulu, nokungabinandaba noNkulunkulu, nangokungaphili impilo ephelele ayibekelwe nguNkulunkulu.
People are Separated UMUNTU UHLUKENE NONKULUNKULU
"The wages of sin is death" [spiritual separation from God]. (Romans 6:23) “Ngokuba inkokhelo yesono ingukufa” [ukwahlukana komoya noNkulunkulu] Roma 6:23
God is holy and man is sinful. There is a great separation between the two. The arrows show how man tries to reach God and the abundant life through his own efforts: such as living a good life, being religious, helping others etc., but all his efforts fail. UNkulunkulu uNgcwele. Umuntu uyisoni. Kukhona isikhulu phakathi kwabo. We Receive Christ by Personal Invitation UKRISTU SIMAMUKELA NGOKUMUVUMELA UKUBA ANGENE EZIMPILWENI ZETHU
[Christ speaking] "Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if anyone hears My voice and opens the door, I will come in to him." (Revelation 3:20) [Ukristu wathi] “Bheka ngimi ngasemnyango ngiyangqongqotha; uma umuntu ezwa izwi lami avule umnyango ngiyakungena kuye…” (Isambulo 3:20).
Receiving Christ involves turning to God from self (repentance) and trusting Christ to come into our lives to forgive our sins and to make us what He wants us to be. Just to agree intellectually that Jesus Christ is the Son of God and that He died on the cross for our sins is not enough. Nor is it enough to have an emotional experience. We receive Jesus Christ by faith, as an act of the will. Ukwamukela uKristu kusho ukuphendukela kuNkulunkulu nokwethemba uKristu ukuba angene ezimpilweni zethu axolele izono zethu. Asenze lokhu athanda ukuba sibe yikho. Ukwamukela ngokuhlakanipha ukuthi uJesu Kristu uyindodana kaNkulunkulu nokuthi wafa esiphambanweni ngenxa yezono zethu akwanele. Futhi akwanele ukwamukela ngemizwa. Samukela uJesu Kristu ngokukholwa njengesenzo sokuvuma.

You Can Receive Christ Right Now by Faith Through Prayer UNGAMAMUKELA UKRISTU KHONA MANJE
(Prayer is talking with God) (Ukukhuleka ukukhuluma noNkulunkulu)
God knows your heart and is not so concerned with your words as He is with the attitude of your heart. The following is a suggested prayer: Mcele ukuba angene empilweni yakho. Methembe ukuthi uzokufeza lokho akuthembisileyo. Yena unaka kakhulu okufiswa yinhliziyo yakho kunokushiwo ngamawzi omlomo wakho. Ungakhuleka ngalendlela elandelayo:
“Lord Jesus, I need You. I have been controlling my own life and have sinned against You. Thank You for forgiving my sins. Come into my life and take control of my life. Make me the kind of person You want me to be.” “Nkosi Jesu, ngiyakudinga. Kade ngiziphathele mina ukuphila kwami ngalokho ngonile kuwe. Ngiyabonga ukuthi ungithethelele izono zami. Ngena empilweni yami uyibuse. Ngenze ngibe yilokho othanda ukuba ngibeyikhona”.
Does this prayer express the desire of your heart? If it does, I invite you to pray this prayer right now. Ngabe lomthandazo usho okufiswa yinhliziyo yakho na?
Christ will come into your life as He promised. Uma usho khona, thandaza manje, khona uKristu ezongena empilweni yakho njengokuthembisa kwakhe.
HOW TO KNOW THAT CHRIST IS IN YOUR LIFE UNGAZI KANJANI UKUTHI UKRISTU USESEMPILWENI YAKHO?
Did you receive Christ into your life? According to His promise in Revelation 3:20, where is Christ right now in relation to you? Christ said that He would come into your life. Would He mislead you? On what authority do you know that God has answered your prayer? (The trustworthiness of God Himself and His Word.) Usumamukele yini uKristu empilweni yakho? Ngokwesithembiso sakhe esitholakala ku Isambulo 3:20, uphi uKristu manje na? UKristu uthembise ukuthi uNkulunkulu usewuphendulile umthandzo wakho? (Ngokwithemba uNkulunkulu neZwi lakhe).

"God has given us eternal life, and this life is in His Son. He who has the Son has the life; he who does not have the Son of God does not have the life. (1 John 5:11-13) “UNkulunkulu usipha ukuphila okuphakade, nalokhukuphila kuseNdodaneni yakhe. Onayo iNdodana kaNkulunkulu ayunakho ukuphila. Nginilobele lokho, ukuze nazi ukuthi nikuphila okuphakade nina enikholwa egameni leNdodana kaNKulunkulu”. (1 Johane 5:11-13)
Thank God often that Christ is in your life and that He will never leave you (Hebrews 13:5). You can know on the basis of His promise that Christ lives in you and that you have eternal life from the very moment you invite Him in. He will not deceive you. Mbonge njalo uNkulunkulu ngoba uKristu usesempilweni yakho. Ushilo ukuthi akasoze akushiye (KumaHeberu 13:5). Usungazi ukuthi uKristu uphila ngaphakathi kuwena nokuthi unokuphila okuphakade ngenxa yesethembiso sakhe.

Do Not rely on your Feelings UNGAHAMBI NGEMIZWA
The promise of God's Word, the Bible - not our feelings - is our authority. The Christian lives by faith (trust) in the trustworthiness of God Himself and His Word. This train diagram illustrates the relationship among fact (God and His Word), faith (our trust in God and His Word), and feeling (the result of our faith and obedience). (Read John 14:21) Isithembiso sezwi likaNkulunkulu siyigunya lethu, hayi imizwa yethu. Ikholwa liphila ngokukholwa (ngokwethemba) ekwethembekeni kuka Nkulunkulu nezwi lakhe, imizwa (umvuso wokukholwa kwethu noku lalela) (Funda u Johane 14:21).
The train will run with or without a passenger car. However, it would be useless to attempt to pull the train by the passenger car. In the same way, as Christians we do not depend on feelings or emotions to decide what is true, but we place our faith (trust) in the trustworthiness of God and the promises of His Word. Lesitimela sizohamba nendlu yomshayeli ngaphandle kwendlu yomshayeli. Kungeke kwasiza ukuzama ukudonsa isitimela ngendlu yomshayeli. Njengamakholwa asithembeli.Emizweni kodwa sibeka ukukholwa kwethu (ithemba) ekwethembekeni kuka Nkulunkulu nasezithembisweni zeZwi lakhe.

SUGGESTIONS FOR CHRISTIAN GROWTH UNGAKHULA KANJANI ENKOSINI
Spiritual growth results from trusting Jesus Christ. Kudingekile ukuba ukhule ekukholweni kwakho. Lokhu okulandelayo kuzokusiza ukuba ukhule.
  • Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7)
  • Khuleka kuNkulunkulu njalo (Johane 15:7).
  • Read God's Word daily. (see Acts 17:11) Begin with the Gospel of John.
  • Funda iZwi likaNkulunkulu njalo (Izenzo 17:11) qale ngo Johane.
  • Obey God moment by moment. (see John 14:21)
  • Lalela uNkulunkulu njalo (Johane 14:21).
  • Witness for Christ by your life and words. (see Matthew 4:19 and John 15:8)
  • Fakazela uKristu ngempilo nangamazwi akho (Matewu 4:19; Johane 15:8).
  • Trust God for every detail of your life (1 Peter 5:7).
  • Ncika kuNkulunkulu kuyo yonke into eqondene nokuphila kwakho (I Petru 5:7).
  • Have God control and empower your daily life and witness (Galatians 5:16 & Acts 1:8).
  • Vumela uMoya oNgcwele ukuba akubuse, akuphe amandla yonke imihla ukuze ube-ngufakazi kaKristu (KwabaseGalathiya 5:16,17; Izenzo 1:8).
