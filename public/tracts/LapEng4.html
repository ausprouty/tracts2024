Have you heard of the Four Spiritual Laws? Wali owulidde ku nsonga enkulu ennya ?
Just as there are physical laws that govern the physical universe, so are there spiritual laws that govern your relationship with God. Tumanyi nti waliwo ensonga ezifuga enkolagana yaffe n’abantu bannaffe. Bwe kityo waliwo ensonga ezitakyuka ezifuga enkolagana yaffe ne Katonda. Katonda ayagala tumanye engeri gye tusaanidde okukolagana.
1

LAW ONE

God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

1

ENSONGA ESOOKA

KATONDA AKWAGALA ERA YAKUTONDA OBEERE N’OBULAMU OBW’EMIREMBE N’ESSANYU.

God's Love OKWAGALA KWA KATONDA
"For God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish but have eternal life." (John 3:16, NIV) “Katonda yayagala nnyo ensi, n’okuwaayo n’awaayo Omwana we omu yekka, buli muntu yenna amukkiriza aleme okuzikirira, naye abeere n’obulamu obutaggwaawo.” (Yokaana 3:16)
God's Plan OBULAMU OBW’EMIREMBE N’ESSANY U
[Christ speaking] “I came that they might have life, and might have it abundantly” [that it might be full and meaningful]. (John 10:10) “Nze najja mube n’obulamu era mube nabwo nga bujjuvu.” [obulamu obujjudde emirembe n’essanyu nga bya mazima]. (Yokaana 10:10).
Why is it that most people are not experiencing the abundant life? Because... Lwaki abantu abasinga obungi tebalina bulamu buno obujjuvu?
Because… Затoа штo...
2

LAW TWO

People are SINFUL and SEPARATED from God so we cannot know and experience God’s love and plan for our life.

2

LAW TWO

OMUNTU YAYONOONA ERA YAYA-WUKANA NE KATONDA. N’OLW’EKYO TAYINZA KUBEERA MU KWAGALA KWA KATONDA NEWANKUBADDE OKUFUNA OBULAMU OBW’-EMIREMBE N’ESSANYU.

People are Sinful OMUNTU YAYONOONA
"All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23) “Abantu bonna baayonoona ne batatuuka ku kitiibwa kya Katonda.” (Abaruumi 3:23)
We were created to have a personal relationship with God, but by our own choice and self-will we have gone our own independent way and that relationship has been broken. This self-will, often seen as an attitude of active rebellion towards God or a lack of interest in Him, is an evidence of what the Bible calls sin. Ekibi kye ki? Ekibi kwe kulemwa okukola ebyo Katonda by’ayagala, era kwe kujeemera Katonda. Omuntu yatondebwa abeere n’omukwano ne Katonda, naye olw’emputtu ye n’okwagala okukola ebibye, yakyamira mu kkubo lye ye, omukwano gwe ne Katonda ne guggwaawo.
People are Separated OMUNTU YAYAWUKANA NE KATONDA
"The wages of sin is death" [spiritual separation from God]. (Romans 6:23) “Empeera y’ekibi kwe kufa.” [omuntu okwawukanira ddala ne Katonda n’okubonaabona okw’emi rembe n’emirembe] (Abaruumi 6:23)
This picture illustrates that God is holy and people are sinful. A great gap separates the two. The arrows illustrate that people are continually trying to reach God and the abundant life through their own efforts, such as a good life, philosophy, or religion – but they always fail. Katonda mutukuvu, ate omuntu mwonoonefu, Olukonko olunene lubaawudde bombi, Ekifaananyi kino kiraga omuntu ng’agezaako ye yekka okutuuka eri Katonda. Oluusi yeegendereza mu by’eddiini, oluusi afuba okubeera omuntu omulungi, era oluusi asinza balubaale.
The third law explains the only way to bridge this gap… Naye, nga bw’olaba, byonna tebimuyamba.
3

LAW THREE

Jesus Christ is God’s ONLY provision for our sin. Through Him alone you can know and experience God’s love and plan for your life.

3

ENSONGA EY'OKUSATU

YESU YE YEKKA AYINZA OKUGGYAWO EKIBI KYAFFE. AYAGALA OKUTUKOMYAWO MU KWAGALA KWA KATONDA N’OKUTUWA OBULAMU OBW’EMIREMBE N’ESSANYU.

He Died in Our Place YESU YEEWAAYO OKUFA KU LWAFFE
"God demonstrates His own love toward us, in that while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us." (Romans 5:8) “Katonda alaga okwagala kwe gye tuli, kubanga bwe twali nga tukyali boonoonyi, Kristo y’atufiirira.” (Abaruumi 5:8)
He Rose From the Dead YESU MULAMU LEERO
“…who through the Spirit of holiness was declared with power to be the Son of God by his resurrection from the dead: Jesus Christ our Lord. (Romans 1:4). “Yesu Kristo yalagibwa okuba Omwana wa Katonda mu maanyi, bwe yazuukira okuva mu bafu.” (Abaruumi 1:4)
He Is the Only Way to God YESU LYE KKUBO LYOKKA
"Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, and the truth, and the life; no one comes to the Father but through Me.'" (John 14:6) Yesu n’amugamba nti “Nze kkubo, n’amazima, n’obulamu; tewali ajja eri Kitange wabula ng’ayita mu nze.” (Yokaana 14:6)
This diagram illustrates that God has bridged the gulf that separates us from Him by sending His Son, Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place to pay the penalty for our sins. Katonda bwe yaweereza Yesu okufa ku musaalaba ku Iwaffe, yatinda olukonko olutwawula naye. Nga bw’olaba mu kifaananyi, kati tuli ba ddembe okuddayo gy’ali.
It is not enough just to know these three laws... Naye okumanya obumanya ensonga zino essatu ekimala....

We must individually RECEIVE Jesus Christ as Saviour and Lord; then we can know and experience God’s love and plan for our lives.

KITUGWANIRA buli muntu ku lulwe okusembeza yesu ng’omulokozi we. Bwe tumukkiriza okufuga obulamu bwaffe, ajja kutukomyawo mu kwagala kwa katonda era atuwe obulamu obw’emirembe n’essanyu.

We Must Receive Christ KITUGWANIRA OKUSEMBEZA KRISTO
"As many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, even to those who believe in His name." (John 1:12) “Bonna abaamusembeza yabawa obuyinza okufuuka abaana ba Katonda, be bakkiriza erinnya lye.” (Yokaana 1:12)
We Receive Christ Through Faith TUMUSEMBEZA MU KUKKIRIZA
"By grace you have been saved through faith; and that not of yourselves, it is the gift of God; not as a result of works so that no one may boast." (Ephesians 2:8,9) “Mwalokoka Iwa kisa Iwa kukkiriza; so tekwava gye muli; kye kirabo kya Katonda; tekwava mu bikolwa, omuntu yenna aleme okwenyumirizanga.” (Abaefeso 2:8,9)
We Receive Christ by Personal Invitation TUMUSEMBEZA BWE TUMWANIRIZA MU BULAMU BWAFFE
[Christ speaking] “Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if any one hears My voice and opens the door, I will come in to him.” (Revelation 3:20) [Kristo ayogera] “Laba nnyimiridde ku luggi, nneeyanjula; omuntu yenna bw’awulira eddoboozi Iyange, n’aggulawo oluggi, nnayingira gy’ali.” (Okubikkulirwa 3:20)
TO ACCEPT JESUS CHRIST IS: OKUSEMBEZA YESU KITEGEEZA:
  1. To acknowledge that you are a sinner, repent and agree that Jesus died on the cross in your place.
  2. To believe that God has forgiven you all yoour sins.
  3. To allow Jesus Christ to be in charge of your whole life so that you can live the way God wants you to live.
  4. We do not do this by merely speaking or just thinking about it.

You Can Receive Christ Right Now by Faith Through Prayer OYINZA OKUSEMBEZA KRISTO KAAKATI WANO MU KUSABA.
(Prayer is talking with God) (Okusaba kwe kwogera ne Katonda).)
God knows your heart and is not so concerned with your words as He is with the attitude of your heart. The following is a suggested prayer: Katonda amanyi omutima gwo; ebigambo byo tabifaako nnyo okusinga bw’afaayo ku ebyo by’alaba mu mutima gwo. Oyinza okusaba bw’oti:
Lord Jesus, I need You. Thank you for dying on the cross for my sins. I open the door of my life and receive You as my Savior and Lord. Thank You for forgiving my sins and giving me eternal life. Take control of the throne of my life. Make me the kind of person You want me to be. “Ayi Yesu, nsonyiwa ebibi byange. Nkuggulirawo oluggi olw’obulamu bwange. Twala obulamu bwange obufuge era onfuule omuntu omuggya. Nkwebaza. Ayi Mukama, olw’okuwulira okusaba kwange n’okuyingira mu bulamu bwange, nga bwe wasuubiza.”
Does this prayer express the desire of your heart? If it does, I invite you to pray this prayer right now. Okusaba kuno kukuyamba okwanjulira Katonda ebiri mu mutima gwo?
Christ will come into your life as He promised. Obanga bwe kiri, saba mu bigambo ebyo, ne Kristo anaayingira mu bulamu bwo nga bwe yasuubiza.
The Bible promises Eternal Life to all who receive Christ. ABAYINGIZA YESU KATONDA ABASUUBIZA OKUBAWA OBULAMU OBUTAGGWAAWO.
“The witness is this, that God has given us eternal life, and this life is in His Son. He who has the Son has the life; he who does not have the Son of God does not have life. These things I have written to you who believe in the name of the Son of God, in order that you may know that you have eternal life” (1 John 5:11–13). “Katonda yatuwa obulamu obutaggwaawo, era obulamu obwo buli mu Mwana we (Yesu). Alina Omwana alina obulamu; atalina Mwana wa Katonda talina bulamu. Ebyo mbibawandiikidde mmwe, mumanye nga mulina obulamu obutaggwaawo, mmwe abakkiriza erinnya ly’Omwana wa Katonda.” (1 Yokaana 5:11-13)
How to Know That Christ Is in Your Life >ENGERI GY’OMANYAMU NGA KRISTO ALI MU BULAMU BWO
Did you pray to receive Christ into your life? How do you know that God has answered your prayer? (I know because of the faithfulness of God and His Word.) Thank God always because Christ is in your life and he will never leave you (Matt 28:20). You have eternal life from the very moment you received Christ, based on his promise. He could not lie to you. Kristo yasuubiza okuyingira mu bulamu bwo, Ddala ddala omusabye okukikola? N’olw’ekyo kati ali ludda wa? Omanyira ku ki nti Katonda azzeemu okusaba kwo? (Mmanyira ku bwesigwa bwa Katonda n’Ekigambo kye), Weebaze Katonda bulijjo kabanga Kristo ali mu bulamu bwo, era tajja kukuleka n’akatono (Matayo 28:20). Olina obulamu obutaggwaawo okuviira ddala ku kaseera ke wasemberezzaamu Kristo, ng’osinziira ku kisuubizo kye. Taayinza kukulimba.
DO NOT DEPEND ON FEELINGS NTEEKWA KUWULIRA NTYA MU BULAMU BWANGE NDYOKE NKAKASE?
The promise of God's Word, the Bible - not our feelings - is our authority. The Christian lives by faith (trust) in the trustworthiness of God Himself and His Word. This train diagram illustrates the relationship among fact (God and His Word), faith (our trust in God and His Word), and feeling (the result of our faith and obedience). (Read John 14:21) Teweesiga nkyukakyuka y’ebirowoozo byo oba embeera y’omubiri gwo, Ekisuubizo ky’Ekigambo kya Katonda bwe bukakafu bwaffe, sso ssi kwewulira kwa mu birowoozo oba mu mubiri, Omukristaayo abeera mulamu Iwa kukkiriza mu bwesigwa bwa Katonda ye nnyini n’Ekigambo kye, Ekifaananyi kino eky’eggaali y’omukka kituwa ekyokulabirako wakati w’AMAZlMA (Katonda n’Ekigambo kye), OKUKKIRIZA (okwesiga Katonda n’Ekigambo kye), n’EMBEERA Y’OBULAMU (eva mu kukkiriza n’obuwulize bwaffe) (Yokaana 14:21)
It would be useless to attempt to pull the train by the guard's van. In the same way, as Christians we do not depend on feelings or emotions, but we place our faith (trust) in the trustworthiness of God and the promises of His Word. Yingini y’eggaali y’omukka y’esika ebiyumba, naye ebiyumba tebiyinza n’akatono kusika yingini wadde okutambula byokka. Mu ngeri y’emu, ffe ng’Abakristaayo tutwesiga mbeera ya bulamu bwaffe, naye tukkiriza ne twesiga Katonda n’ebisuubizo by’Ekigambo kye
...You have become a child of God. Nothing can change this truth. You may feel like you do not belong God; but this cannot change the fact of what God has done in your life. …Ofuuse omwana wa Katonda. Tewali kiyinza kukyusa mazima gano. Oyinza okuwulira ng’atali mwana wa Katonda; naye kino tekiyinza kukyusa ekyo Katonda ky’akoze mu bulamu bwo.
Now That You Have Received Christ KAAKATI NG’OMAZE OKUYINGIZA KRISTO MU BULAMU BWO
The moment you received Christ by faith, as an act of the will, many things happened, including the following: Obanga oyingizizza Kristo mu bulamu bwo, ebintu bingi ebikubaddeko. Bino bye bimu ku byo:
  • Christ came into your life (Revelation 3:20)
  • Kristo ayingidde mu bulamu bwo. (Okubikkulirwa 3:20)
  • Your sins were forgiven (Colossians 1:14)
  • Osonyiyiddwa ebibi byo. (Abakkolosaayi 1:14)
  • You became a child of God (John 1:12)
  • Ofuuse omwana wa Katonda. (Yokaana 1:12)
  • You began the great adventure for which God created you (John 10:10, ).
  • Otandise obulamu obw’ekitalo Katonda bwe yakutondera. (Yokaana 10:10)
Can you think of anything more wonderful that could happen to you than receiving Christ? Would you like to thank God in prayer right now for what He has done for you? By thanking God, you demonstrate your faith. Olowooza otya? Waliwo ekisinga okubeera ne Kristo mu bulamu bwo? Wandyagadde okwebaza Katonda kaakati wano, olw’ebyo by’akukoledde bw’ayingidde mu bulamu bwo? Ekikolwa kye nnyini eky’okwebaza Katonda kiraga okukkiriza kwo.
Suggestions for Christian Growth ENGERI GY’OKULAMU OKUBA OMUKRISTAAYO OW’AMAANYI
Spiritual growth results from trusting Jesus Christ. "...The righteous man shall live by faith" (Galatians 3:11). A life of faith will enable you to trust God increasingly with every detail of your life, and to practice the following: Okukula mu bulamu obw’omwoyo kuva mu kwesiga Yesu Kristo. “Abatuukirivu banaabeeranga balamu lwa kukkiriza.” (Abaggalatiya 3:11) Obulamu obw’okukkiriza bujja kukusobozesa okukula mu kwesiga Katonda mu kwetaaga kwonna okw’obulamu bwo, era ng’okola bino:
  • Go to God in prayer daily (John 15:7)
  • NYUMYA NE KATONGA bulijjo mu kusaba. (Yokaana 15:7)
  • Read God's Word daily. (see Acts 17:11) Begin with the Gospel of John.
  • SOMA EKIGAMBO KYA KATONDA buli kaseera (Ebikolwa by’Abatume 17:11), Oyinza okutandikira ku Njiri ya Yokaana.
  • Obey God moment by moment. (see John 14:21)
  • GONDERA KATONDA buli kaseera. (Yokaana 14:21)
  • Witness for Christ by your life and words. (see Matthew 4:19 and John 15:8)
  • TEGEEZA ABALALA KU YESU mu bikolwa ne mu bigambo byo. (Matayo 4:19; Yokaana 15:8)
  • Trust God for every detail of your life. (1 Peter 5:7)
  • WEESIGE KATONDA mu bulamu bwo bwonna.(1 Peetero 5:7)
  • God’s Word instructs us not to forsake “the assembling of ourselves together” (Hebrews 10:25).
  • WEEGATTE N’ABANTU ABALALA abasembezezza. Yesu era abamwagala. (Abaebbulaniya 10:25)
