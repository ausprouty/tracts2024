"Some however did accept Him; so He gave them the right to become God’s children." John 1:12 “Tiamsis muaj tej txhia txais Nws; ces Nws muab cai rau lawv ua Vajtswv cov menyuas.” Yauhas 1:12
GOD’S GRACE SAVES US THROUGH FAITH VAJTSWV TXOJ KOOBHMOOV CAWM PEB DHAU LOS NTAWM KEV NTSEEG.
"It is by God’s grace that you have been saved, through faith (that trusts Him). It is not through your own effort. It is God’s gift." Ephesians 2:8 “Nws yog Vajtswv txoj koobhmoov nej thiaj tau dim, dhau los ntawm kev ntseeg. Nws tsis yog dhau los ntawm nej tus dag zog. Nws yog Vajtswv yam khoom pub.” Efexaus 2:8
WE SHOW OUR BELIEF IN JESUS CHRIST BY ACCEPTING HIS INVITATION PEB NTSEEG YEXUS KHETOS LOS NTAWM KEV TXAIS NWS LO LUS CAW
Jesus Christ said, “Listen, I stand at the door (of your heart) and knock; if anyone hears my voice and opens the door, I will come in to him (your heart).” Revelation 3:20 Yexus Khetos haistias, “Mloog, Kuv sawv ntawm qhovrooj (koj lub siab) thiab khob; yog leejtwg hnov Kuv lub suab hu thiab qhib lub qhovrooj, Kuv yuav los rau hauv nws (lub siab).” Tshwmsim 3:20
To believe in Jesus Christ means to follow God’s way instead of our own way, to trust Christ to come into our lives, to forgive our sins and make us what He wants us to be. It is not enough to agree in our minds that what God wants is true or to have special feelings of being happy or sad when we think about these things. yog taug yus txojkev, peb yuav tau ntseeg Yexus rau peb lub neej, ua tus zam peb tej kev txhaum thiab cia Nws hloov peb li uas Nws pom zoo. OB LUB VUJ NUAV QHIA TXOG OB LUB NEEJ:
(THESE TWO CIRCLES SHOW TWO KINDS OF LIVES):

Yus losyog kuv yog tus tswj
(Self or me in control.)

Khetos yog tus tswj.
(Christ is in control.)

  • Kuv yog tus Tswv. (I am the Lord).
  • Kuv ua txhua yam uas Kuv xav ua. (I do the things I want to do).
  • Khetos nyob sab nraum kuv lub neej. (Christ is outside my life).
  • Khetos yog tug Tswv. (Christ is the Lord).
  • Kuv ua txhua yam uas Nwg hais kom kuv ua. (I do what He wants me to do).
  • Kuv muaj lub neej txaus siab heev. (I have a life that is satisfying.)
  • Kuv yog Vajtswv ib tus phoojywg. (We live a life with God as our friend.).

Tamsim no koj lub neej yog lub twg?
(Which kind of life do you have now? )

Lub neej twg koj xav tau?
(Which kind of life do you want to have?)
